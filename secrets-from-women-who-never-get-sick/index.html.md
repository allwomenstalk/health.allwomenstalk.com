# Secrets to Remember from Women Who Never Get Sick 🤒 ...

You don't want to spend the season sneezing and coughing. That's why you should follow [Good Housekeeping's](https://www.goodhousekeeping.com/health/g2829/secrets-from-women-who-never-get-sick/) advice and use these secrets from women who never get sick:

## 1. Have a Set Bedtime

![Have a Set Bedtime](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ac/ya/smdozke65617d15b7c12e397831512_600x399.jpg)

Make sure you get eight hours of sleep per night.

## 2. Eat Antioxidant-rich Foods

![Eat Antioxidant-rich Foods](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ok/zb/tkenv7775617d15bde339042737134_600x450.jpg)

These are foods like sweet potatoes, citrus, almonds, and red grapes.

## 3. Take a Probiotic Supplement

![Take a Probiotic Supplement](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lg/qp/mdbravna5617d15cc76d3950698867_600x400.jpg)

They contain helpful bacteria, which can boost your immune system.

## 4. Wash Your Hands

![Wash Your Hands](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b1/fz/wx7rkd5b5617d1611d116084590919_600x441.jpg)

Don't do so constantly, just do it before you eat, after you prepare food, after you use the bathroom, and after you sneeze or cough.

## 5. Get Social Support

![Get Social Support](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wo/uv/q8nu4r9m5617d15c622ee393058174_600x400.jpg)

When you're feeling stressed, hang out with your best group of friends

## 6. Practice Yoga

![Practice Yoga](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eo/ms/p0w2y00b5617d15c8641a341644170_600x400.jpg)

Breathing exercises can strengthen your immune system, you know.
