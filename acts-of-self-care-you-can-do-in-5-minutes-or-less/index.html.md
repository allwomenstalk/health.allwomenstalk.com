# 12 Acts of Self Care You Can do in 5 Minutes  or Less ...

When people talk about self-care, some of the stuff that you hear about the most is big scale actions like huge lifestyle changes, travel, lots of time off of work ... generally, things that take a little bit of fore-planning and can’t be done at the drop of a hat. The whole point of self care though, in my opinion, is doing things that can help to start making you feel better in a really short space of time, not waiting until you have enough money or enough time to ‘do it big’. Here are twelve **acts of self-care you can do in five minutes or less**.

## 1. Breathe

![shoulder, girl, fun, smile, long hair,](https://img.allw.mn/content/yx/zh/zdid4s3n5bacc4512f240919706153_480x270.gif)

It’s something that you are doing all the time, but not something that you are concentrating on! Take a minute to shut your eyes and really focus on your breathing, taking nice deeps breaths to reduce your heart rate and relax your muscles.

## 2. Write

![text, book, font, hand, writing,](https://img.allw.mn/content/sr/f5/yxm5uup55bacc497a38a2473945863_500x214.gif)

A good way to relieve stress and get it out of your body is to write it down. Keeping a journal can really help to unravel your emotions.

## 3. Meditate

![sky, morning, girl, sun, sunlight,](https://img.allw.mn/content/o8/zf/jechdc9f5bacc4ee92644342196505_540x203.gif)

Meditation isn’t only for hippies. The best thing to do right now is download one of the many apps that will guide through a daily routine.

## 4. Elevate Legs

![cartoon, yellow, text, art, line,](https://img.allw.mn/content/s1/fb/vf5jp09a5bacc6ba1b7bd401326648_720x405.gif)

If you have the time to lay down somewhere, do so with your legs elevated up along the side of a wall. It’s a yoga pose that can instantly relieve stress and tension within your body.

## 5. Freshen up

![pink, fashion, magenta, girl, long hair,](https://img.allw.mn/content/at/me/idm157ix5bacc7059198d053171061_480x264.gif)

Do a few little things to freshen yourself up quickly, like brush your teeth, wash your face, wash your hair, apply some perfume, anything that can give you a boost.

## 6. Get outside

![girl, fur,](https://img.allw.mn/content/nn/li/emu2qyot5bacc76fde45d859686806_480x270.gif)

Spend a few minutes in the great outdoors, whether it’s taking a quick walk around your nearest park or sitting in the garden to have your cup of coffee rather than in front of the TV.

## 7. Music

![face, skin, hairstyle, chin, cheek,](https://img.allw.mn/content/jd/en/anjtlqrd5bacc80ed7750774767212_480x270.gif)

Don’t underestimate the curative powers of music. Put on your favourite playlist and lose yourself in the rhythm.

## 8. Dance

![pink, beauty, girl, fun, black hair,](https://img.allw.mn/content/rx/y5/zqmpioc95bacc84ab8821532950705_640x360.gif)

And while the music is playing, make sure that you move your body in time! Dancing is a super fun release.

## 9. Hug

![photograph, black and white, monochrome photography, photography, snapshot,](https://img.allw.mn/content/b5/v5/xddrvyf35bacc88c3c99a357985110_415x176.gif)

Hug someone that you love, and hold on to them for a little longer than you normally would. That human connection can be really restorative and special.

## 10. Make Plans

![girl, forehead, black hair, long hair, product,](https://img.allw.mn/content/vq/ej/urffmc0c5bacc8cdb6b00256893094_500x258.gif)

Give yourself something to look forward to by making plans, either with friends for the weekend, or for something bigger like a party or a vacation in the future.

## 11. Fantasize

![hair, blond, human hair color, eyebrow, head,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ys/xu/tbohbgvj5bacc92907c2f543640298_500x281.gif)

Indulge in your daydreams and allow them to take over for a little bit every day. It can be fun to live a fantasy life on your lunch break!

## 12. Play with Pets

![cat, small to medium sized cats, cat like mammal, whiskers, fur,](https://img.allw.mn/content/xq/fw/xht9ah6c5bacc9e4b025e858106647_500x281.gif)

If you have a pet around the house, make more of an effort to play with them. The calming effects of stroking a dog or a cat, or even a rabbit, have been well documented!
