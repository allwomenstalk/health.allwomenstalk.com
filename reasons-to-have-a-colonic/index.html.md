# 9 Reasons to Have a Colonic ...

There are some good reasons to have a colonic, but as people find out more and more about the therapy there is still much controversy surrounding the treatment. Personal reasons to have colonic hydrotherapy include weight loss and treatment of fatigue. Health reasons to have colonic irrigation include liver cleansing and other benefits. If you are interested but would like to know more, here are 9 Reasons to Have a Colonic.

## 1. Ancient Practice

Colon cleansing is an age old practice that has been around for thousands of years, with evidence of the treatment occurring in respected ancient medicinal and health practices from all over the world. Colon cleansing is considered to be a respected practice with roots found in Ancient Egypt, age old Ayurvedic medicine and ancient Chinese medicinal practices. These ancient practices are still revered today as natural therapies and provide the top reasons to have a colonic.

## 2. Toxin Cleansing

There are so many misconceptions about colon therapy and the cleansing process, that there are many people who don’t fully comprehend all of the benefits, which are documented to be far reaching. It is not only a slow digestive system that makes on feel sluggish, it is the toxins in our bodies that contributes to the low energy as well. Detox diets can be long and difficult to endure, giving more reasons to have colonic irrigation which is a much faster one-off detoxifying treatment.

## 3. Reflex Stimulation

Part of the colon hydrotherapy treatment includes the use of warm water that is passed through the colon. This part of the treatment process is one of the best reasons to have colonic hydrotherapy as the warm and tepid water stimulates the reflexes in the colon area, which is like a gentle and soothing massage. Anyone who suffers with difficult bowel movements should see improvement after a colonic.

## 4. Muscle Strength

The colon is a muscle and one that becomes weak and lazy over time, especially for those people who have a sluggish digestive system. This may mean relying on laxatives to aid digestion. The use of laxatives will make the colon lazier and lazier, to the point that the colon is unable to do its job without them. One of the best reasons to have colon irrigation treatment is to help the muscles retain their strength.

## 5. Reset the Body Clock

With modern lifestyles, processed foods, alcohol, tobacco, stress, bad diet and medications, the modern body is under strain. We work hard to fight against their natural rhythms and body clock, in order to perform better under pressure, to work harder, stay awake longer and go way beyond our limits. A colonic will help to reset the body clock and will be beneficial to general health.

## 6. Balance Moods

There is no question that a slow and lazy digestive system affects our moods, as the uncomfortable symptoms of digestive problems interfere with day to day life. Medications and laxatives provide short interim periods of relief, but on a long term basis, the problems still persist and the side effects of the medication will only add to the problem. Another of the sound reasons to have a colonic is to balance your mood, leading to a happier life.

## 7. Healing

A blocked digestive system can be the cause of many toxins building up in the body. Harbouring toxins not only slow down the digestive system, but that start to permeate the rest of the body as well. Anybody suffering from an illness will find the treatment of colon hydrotherapy very beneficial to their healing process.

## 8. Chakra Balance

Whether or not you believe that we as humans are full of energy, and that our energy centres transfer or harbour negative and positive energy is of little consequence. You can feel it immediately when your energy levels are affected; you don’t’ necessarily need to understand it. One of the benefits of colon irrigation is that there will be an almost immediate release of negative energy.

## 9. Release Blockages

The average digestive system is said to have blockages of around 3-5kg of waste that has not as yet been expelled from the body, clogging up the digestive system. For people with problems with their digestive systems, this number is indeed higher. It’s hard to argue with reasons to have colonic hydrotherapy if the treatment instantly releases those blockages, helping people feel happier and healthier right away.

Whatever your reasons for having a colonic, there are plenty of health benefits. If you are someone that suffers from digestive issues, you may not need reasons to have colon hydrotherapy, other than you just want relief from the uncomfortable symptoms. Have you ever tried it?
