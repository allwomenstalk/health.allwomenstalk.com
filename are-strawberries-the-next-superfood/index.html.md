# Are Strawberries the Next Superfood? ...

Personally, I could eat strawberries all day, every day. They're jam-packed with nutrients and have a wide array of health benefits. Here's what's so good about this awesome berry:

## 1. Antioxidants

Strawberries have been ranked the 3rd best source of antioxidants among all US foods!

## 2. Cardiovascular Benefits

Strawberries' rich array of vitamins decreases circulating fats and lowers cholesterol levels!

## 3. Blood Sugar Levels

Did you know that strawberries have a moderating effect on blood sugar levels?

## 4. Cancer Prevention

Phytonutrients in strawberries have been shown to reduce the risk of breast, cervical, colon, and esophageal cancer.

## 5. Weight Loss

Anthocyanins in strawberries makes the body burn stored fat at a faster rate! The fact that strawberries are very low in calories (just 54 calories in a cup) is a huge bonus.
