# Look Here for Fast Natural Weight Loss ...

Are you looking for some **natural ways to lose weight quickly**? Want to lose weight fast, but you aren’t sure about weight loss supplements? Want to try a more natural approach for when you go semi-natural? Then maybe detox water is for you.

So what is detox water? It is an infused water that improves your health by flushing out the toxins from your body, thus boosting your metabolism, which will help you lose weight, as well as give you more energy. So here are some delicious detoxes to help get you started on the road to learning more about **natural ways to lose weight quickly**.

## 1. The Original Day Spa Apple Cinnamon

This detox consists of sliced apples (calls for Fuji but it's your preference) and a cinnamon stick, not ground cinnamon.

## 2. The Slimdown

Bring on the citrus and the vitamin C with a mix of lemons, limes, grapefruit, and cucumber to balance this tangy detox. This detox is also full of antioxidants and protects “against immune system deficiencies, cardiovascular disease, prenatal health problems, eye disease, and even skin wrinkling.”

## 3. Blueberry and Orange

Who’d have thought blueberries and oranges would be a cute combination? I didn’t. Not only does this detox look good in a glass, it’s packed full of vitamin C and tones your skin. Plus, it has plenty of antioxidants and fiber, that help curb your appetite.

## 4. Watermelon and Strawberry Mint

Mmm. Minty! If you like a cool, refreshing, and sweet beverage, then this is the detox for you. Watermelons are more than just the summer treat. Watermelons help “lower blood pressure, improve insulin response and alleviate muscle soreness.” And strawberries are full of antioxidants.

## 5. Metabolism Boosting Mango Ginger Water

Now there’s a name. And it has mangoes, my favorite. A blend of fresh (or frozen) mangoes and ginger root, this detox not only boost your metabolism but helps with digestion, memory and increases your libido! Yeah!

## 6. Raspberry Orange

With sliced oranges and lightly crushed raspberries, you’ll have a powerful drink to help fight inflammation, premature aging, and the common cold, as well as the usual vitamin C and antioxidants to help make you healthy.

## 7. Classic Cucumber Water

Now I love me some cucumber water. Cucumber has a refreshing, crisp taste that regular water just can’t beat. Plus, this detox helps you fight inflammation, protect your brain, and even freshens your breath. Who knew!

## 8. Lemon Berry Detox

A blend of blueberries, raspberries, and lemons, this detox will provide you with plenty of vitamin C and antioxidants. Plus, it will change to a crimson/blueish color from the berries and the yellow from the lemons will make it pop, adding a colorful drink to any festive occasion.

## 9. Strawberry, Mint and Lemon Detox

This drink balances the sweetness of the strawberries, the tartness of the lemons, and the coolness of the mint into a party in your mouth. Plus, the colors look awesome in a glass and will be the topic of every conversation at your next gathering.

## 10. The Skinny Detox

This slimming beverage consists of lemons, limes, grapefruit, cucumbers and mint. This contains lots of vitamin C and antioxidants, plus helps boost your metabolism, and it is gorgeous to look at in a pitcher or a glass. You’ll be feeling healthier and slimming down in no time.
