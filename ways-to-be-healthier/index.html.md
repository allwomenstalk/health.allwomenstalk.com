# 8 Ways to Be Healthier ...

**Ways To Be Healthier** are often on our minds, and it´s something that we think about a lot in the early months of the year. We resolve to give up smoking, drink less and exercise more. There are a lot of other ways to be healthier in addition to these classic resolutions. You'll have to be determined and work for it, but it's definitely worth the effort. If you're looking for some great ways to be healthier, here are some tips on improving your health and well-being.

## 1. Fab Food

Eating well is essential if you want to be healthy. Of course, nobody´s perfect, and we all enjoy some treats, so a good principle to follow is the 80/20 rule. Make sure that at least 80% of your diet is healthy, with lots of fresh fruit and vegetables, and then that 20% can be foods that are not so good for you, but fun to eat.

## 2. Activity

Except for sporty readers (or those with small kids to keep them active), most of us could probably do with incorporating more activity into our routine. If you commute to work, have a sedentary job and then spend your leisure time sitting down, you need to find **ways to be healthier**. Moderate activity such as cycling or walking will do that, and don´t need to take up too much time.

## 3. Relaxation

Activity is essential to be healthy, but don´t forget the opposite side of the coin. Relaxation is equally important, when we lead busy and stressful lives. Devote a small part of your free time to relaxing with a book, meditating or enjoying a gentle stroll in a park.

## 4. Banish Bad Habits

Yes, this may be glaringly obvious, but how can you be healthy if you smoke? If you´re aware that you need to find **ways to be healthier**, then it´s time to get rid of bad habits. Smoking is at the top of the list, but bad habits are not just physical. Get rid of any psychological bad habits as well, and your general well-being will improve.

## 5. Stress Reduction

Stress is bad, bad, bad for your health. Not only does it lower your immune system, but have you ever noticed how bad your skin looks when you´re stressed? Your body reacts very negatively to stress factors, so try to remove stress from your life as much as possible, or at least reduce it.

## 6. Positive Outlook

Keeping a positive outlook is very beneficial to your health. Worriers get sick and run down. You don´t have to be bursting with optimism, but do try to cultivate a generally positive way of viewing life. Accept that sometimes things go wrong, and they can become easier to deal with, which is good for your health.

## 7. Super Sleep

Another of the top ways to keep healthy is to get enough sleep. This is essential for the body to repair itself, and for our brains to have a rest. Make sure you get as much sleep as you need. If you have small kids and are short on sleep, try to nap during the day when they do.

## 8. People Power

An often overlooked facet of staying healthy is the contribution that other people make to our lives. We need friends, family and affection around us. People who have a circle of close friends are known to be healthier, as we are social creatures and need the support of others.

There are lots of **ways to be healthier** aside from the obvious. We all need to look after our own health and well-being, and that takes a little bit of effort. It´s all too easy to slip into bad habits, but much harder to break that way of life. But it can be done! What are your favourite ways to be healthy?
