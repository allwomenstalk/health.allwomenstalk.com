# 8 Sound Reasons to Get an Eye Exam ...

Reasons to get an eye exam are plentiful. Unfortunately, this is an aspect of health care that is often neglected. If you haven't had an eye test in the last year or two, then it's time to book one now. Even if you think your eyes are fine, you should still get an eye exam regularly. Here are some important reasons to get your eyes checked ...

## 1. Driving

One of the top reasons to get an eye exam is driving. Not only is it a legal requirement to have good vision, but it's also morally important. It would be wrong to put other people at risk because your eye sight isn't sufficient for driving - and stupid to put yourself at risk.

## 2. Computer Use

Many jobs these days require intense computer use. If you don't already know, it's important to take regular breaks from looking at the screen, and in some circumstances your employers are liable to help you with the cost of glasses. So if you use a computer regularly, get your eyes tested.

## 3. Health Issues

Are you aware that an eye test can detect problems such as high blood pressure, diabetes or high cholesterol? It's very likely that you had no idea that an eye test can show health issues such as these. This is another excellent reason to get an eye exam - the earlier these problems are detected, the better.

## 4. Diabetes

If you are diabetic, you should already be aware that having the condition is one of the reasons to get an eye exam. As I've already mentioned, diabetes is something that can also be determined in an eye test. If you already been diagnosed, make sure that you attend retinal screening appointments and your regular eye tests.

## 5. Unaware of Problems

Another one of the important reasons to get an eye exam is that many eye problems don't actually have symptoms, unlike problems affecting other parts of the body. Since your eyes may not let you know that there's anything wrong with them, an eye test can provide some essential clues.

## 6. Irreplaceable

It's extraordinary how much we take our eyes for granted. But just think how important they are - without perfect vision, life would be very difficult. You can't get a new set of eyes, so look after the ones you have. Regular checkups are the best way to do that, so book that test soon!

## 7. Avoid Eye Strain

If you already wear glasses or contact lenses, you need to be sure that your prescription is up-to-date. Don't assume that it will stay the same. Wearing an incorrect prescription can cause eye strain and headaches. Have an exam as often as your optician recommends.

## 8. Looking after Your Body

Most people think that unless they have obvious problems with their vision, they have no reasons to get an eye exam. Look at it this way - you only have one pair of eyes, and getting a regular test will reassure you that they are working fine. And if they aren't, you will be able to do something about it. See it as "maintenance," just like with a car.

There are so many important reasons to get an eye exam. It's also essential if you wear contact lenses that you are following the correct aftercare procedure and that they are not causing you any problems. Whether you wear glasses or lenses, or neither, always get regular tests. When was the last time you had your eyes checked? If it has been a while I hope this list of reasons to get an eye test has encouraged you to make an appointment soon.
