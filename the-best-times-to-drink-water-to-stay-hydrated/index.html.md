# The Best Times ⏰ of Day to Drink Water to Stay Hydrated ...

Hello everyone! Are you wondering about **the best times to drink water to stay hydrated**? “If there is magic on this planet, it is contained in water.” Loran Eisley, The Immense Journey, 1957.

Drinking enough water is so important and I feel compelled to remind you to drink up and stay well hydrated. And, just in case you need a good reason to drink more water – keep reading!

Drinking water at the correct time increases its effective functions on the human body.
So what are **the best times to drink water to stay hydrated**?
Here’s how to drink water at the correct time to maximizes its effectiveness on your body:

- Drink two glasses of water after waking up – helps activate internal organs.
- Drink one glass of water 30 minutes before a meal – helps digestion.
- Drink one glass of water before taking a bath – helps lower blood pressure.
- Drink one glass of water before sleep – may help to avoid stroke or heart attack.
Benefits of Drinking Water and its Functions in the Body:
- 75% of the brain is water
- Regulates body temperature
- 83% of the body’s blood is water
- Removes body waste
Cushions bone, 75% of muscles are water
- Helps body absorb nutrients
- Protects and cushions vital organ
- Help convert food into energy
- Helps maintain a healthy weight
- Promotes fat loss
- Moistens oxygen for breathing
- Helps carry nutrients and oxygen to cells
- Helps to maintain normal kidney function
- Promotes youthful looking skin - plump, luminous and glowing!
