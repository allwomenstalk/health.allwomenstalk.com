# 7 Benefits of CBD Oil You Need to Know before You Write It off ...

Thanks to certain high profile legalisation of the use of cannabis in certain parts of the world, the recreational drug and all of its various benefits have been more on the mind of late than perhaps they ever have been before. You might immediately associate cannabis with your teenage years, smoking a joint at a party and feeling kind of dizzy afterwards, but that same plant can be used to much better effect in the form of CBD oil! Here are seven of the best **benefits of CBD oil** to consider.

## 1. Pain Relief

Marijuana has been used as a form of pain relief as far back as 2900 B.C., and CBD oil has been developed into lots of different products that can have that effect on the body. From mouth sprays to edibles to things like body lotions that penetrate the skin, the pain relief factor is one of the best when it comes to CBD oil. [Buy CBD Oil](https://www.cbdcentral.com/cbd-ultimate-benefits-guide/) to find relief from persistent pain that modern medicine has no way of treating yet.

## 2. Anxiety and Depression Relief

Anxiety and depression are two extremely prevalent conditions these days, and CBD oil can have a really positive effect in relieving some of the most debilitating symptoms. CBD oil is seen as much more natural approach considering the number of chemical medications that are prescribed for depression on a regular basis.

## 3. Cancer Symptom Relief

CBD oil can’t cure cancer, sadly nothing can in that way - yet -but what it can do is help to treat side effects and symptoms like nausea, vomiting, and, of course, pain. A recent test of cancer patients found that their use of the oil significantly reduced feelings of pain.

## 4. Reduce Acne

Acne is one of the most common skin conditions among the general population, and it is most often caused by an overproduction of sebum in the skin. Scientific studies have shown that CBD oil can help with acne because of its massive anti-inflammatory properties, which will, in turn, reduce the amount of sebum you produce.

## 5. Neuroprotective Properties

There has been recent evidence to suggest that people who suffer from neurological conditions like epilepsy or multiple sclerosis can be helped by CBD oil. Things like oral sprays have been cited as being able to protect neurons in the brain and reduce spasms.

## 6. Heart Health

CBD oil can also benefit a person’s heart health, chiefly for its ability to lower high blood pressure. Along with heart health, the lowering of blood pressure is also another element that can help towards reducing feelings of stress and anxiety.

## 7. Diabetes Prevention

It is a very recent study, but experts in the field not too long ago tested a group of diabetic mice, and their use of CBD oil actually reduced the overall incidence of diabetes by as much as 56%.
