# Perfect Tips to Feel Good Every Day ...

This is a cheat sheet to feel good. Some common sense, maybe a few reminders that you needed.

## 1. Comfy Sneakers

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-2-1767580543221.png)

Your shoes do not have to be expensive! I go to a local thrift store and buy 2 to 3 at a time, it being like Macy's to me. You can often find gently worn to like brand-new.

## 2. Take a Siesta

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-1-1767580546043.png)

Even 20 minutes can give you a little rejuvenation.

## 3. Drink, Drink, Drink

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-0-1767580549021.png)

Whether water (flavored, sparkling, seltzer or fruit-infused), tea (hot or iced), smoothies, or 2 glasses of wine.

## 4. Activity

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-3-1767580551786.png)

Get in at least 10 minutes a day.

## 5. Triphala

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-3-1767580555171.png)

An Ayurvedic capsule to avoid constipation and keep regular. This tones the gastrointestinal tract.

## 6. Talking

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-2-1767580559157.png)

Preserves memory. Tell that to the people who tell you that you talk too much!

## 7. Cranberry Juice

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-0-1767580561871.png)

Stops urinary tract infections.

## 8. No Fast Food

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-1-1767580564856.png)

Cook your own healthy meals.

## 9. Go Outdoors

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-2-1767580567657.png)

Get some of our friend the sun whenever you can.

## 10. Do Something for You

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-1-1767580571120.png)

Raise those feel good hormones in a healthy way.

## 11. Don't Be Negative

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-2-1767580574607.png)

Do something good for someone else. Doing so has proven to make you feel better.

## 12. Sleep

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-0-1767580578397.png)

Can't sleep? Listen to subliminal music on YouTube.

## 13. Portions

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-1-1767580581144.png)

Buy portion plates! This is the easiest way to pay attention to servings.

## 14. Mini-meals

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-0-1767580583917.png)

Don't starve yourself! Good for keeping your blood sugar level. No roller coaster rides.

## 15. Olive Oil

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-3-1767580589887.png)

Extra virgin olive oil is the best fat ever, lowering your risk of heart attack and stroke.

## 16. Food Journaling

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/0-3-1767580593341.png)

Do this if you want to see where adjustments need to be made, especially if you are attempting to lose weight. A good website for this is sparkpeople.com.

These are something to strive for and keep you on track. Do them every day and see the improvement in no time!
