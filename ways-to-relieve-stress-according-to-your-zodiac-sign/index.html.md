# Awesome Ways to Relieve Stress According to Your Zodiac ...

Do you need some **ways to relieve stress according to your zodiac sign**? Astrologist have long studied the different traits associated with the 12 zodiac signs. Individuals with different traits experience the world around them in different ways. For some, such as the Taurus, comfort comes with spending time with those that mean the most. While for others, such as Virgo, a more independent approach to stress relief is suitable. The point is, everyone has different stresses and different ways to relieve that stress. Whether you are a dangerous Aries or a sensitive Pisces, relieving daily stress is a must and it is important to find the right approach for you. So, here are some **ways to relieve stress according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. PISCES (FEBRUARY 19 – MARCH 20)

The Pisces is a kind and sensitive soul that shows sympathy for others daily. They need to escape from the world around them to find peace within themselves. They let their kind nature infringe on their state of mind by caring deeply about others, even when there isn’t anything they can do to help. This creates stress for the Pisces that needs to be released. They can do this by escaping into nature by either camping, hiking, bike riding, horseback riding, etc. If they are stuck indoors, a fantasy book or movie can help them escape reality for a little while.

## 2. Aries (March 21 – April 19)

The Aries is a brave and adventurous soul that wants to experience a sense of danger. A great way to release from the everyday life would be to get out in nature and explore. Going to a nature park and taking a hike or taking a bike ride are great ways for the Aries to get the sense of exploration that their heart desires. If going out isn’t an option, the Aries can always cuddle up with an exciting book or watch a thrilling movie to get that daily dose of excitement.

## 3. Taurus (April 20 – May 20)

The Taurus craves the support of those closest, whether that be a significant other or friends and family. Unwinding from a stressful week could involve cuddling up on the couch with that one person that you just can’t get enough of or going out to eat with friends. Connecting will ease the mind of the Taurus.

## 4. Gemini (May 21- June 20)

The Gemini loves to have fun and is very adaptable to any situation. This means that finding relief from stress could mean a variety of ever-changing interest. Discovering new and exciting activities is great for the Gemini, who is inquisitive in nature and needs new and interesting stimuli. It is helpful for the Gemini to have hobbies or interests to pass the time, thus relieving stress and expanding the mind.

## 5. Cancer (June 21 – July 22)

As with the Taurus, the Cancer craves attention from those that are close to them. Being around loved ones is a great stress reliever for the Cancer. These creative souls need some release as well from everyday life that involves creative expression. This could be watercolors, photography, or writing. Throwing caution to the wind is difficult for the Cancer so they may not want to show anyone their creative side until they are sure of their skill level.

## 6. Leo (July 23 – August 22)

The Leo is another creative soul that leans more toward the perfectionist side. While they will get some stress relief from expanding their creative side, the stress of having to make sure every detail is perfect may not make these activities as effective. With their ever expanding worldview and ability to see situations from every perspective, reading mystery novels is a great way to step out of their hectic life and into a situation that has been crafted to test them. Puzzles or brainteasers are a great way to pass the time and relieve the mind of stress as well.

## 7. Virgo (August 23 – September 22)

The Virgo is a natural worrier. It is important to step away from a problem that is stressful and take an analytical look at all the details. This works for the Virgo because they tend to do better at breaking down a situation and analyzing every piece of information rather than looking at the situation and making a snap judgment. With a modest disposition, crowded environments may create more stress than they are worth. Having some alone time to think and ponder is a great stress reliever for the Virgo.

## 8. Libra (September 23 – October 22)

The Libra is a social butterfly who craves attention. If left alone, the Libra will worry that they are missing out on something. To relieve stress, the Libra needs to be around others and have some fun. Sitting at home and reading a book is rarely what the Libra would find relaxing. They want to go out. They want to be in the middle of the action. Being the mediator for friends is a daily routine and they wouldn’t want it any other way.

## 9. Scorpio (October 23 – November 21)

The Scorpio craves a close relationship with those around them. To relieve the stress of a Scorpio, having a fun date with a significant other or going out with close friends and family is a must. It creates stress to have to share the attention of another. It is important for the Scorpio to stay close to those that are important to them and show them a good time. Stress relief is centered around improving and protecting their relationships with significant others, friends, and family.

## 10. Sagittarius (November 22 – December 21)

The Sagittarius becomes bored very easily and needs constant stimuli or stress builds. When not at work, a Sagittarius needs to be doing something with their hands. They usually have a positive outlook on life but that can deteriorate quickly if they become restless. This restlessness can lead to reckless behavior if not taken care of. The Sagittarius would be wise to find a hobby to occupy their hands and minds. This could be anything from woodworking, needlepoint, writing, or video gaming.

## 11. Capricorn (December 22 – January 19)

The Capricorn is a reserved and caring soul that has a tendency toward being pessimistic and downright gloomy when the right conditions are present. Relieving stress is important for the Capricorn who has a natural inclination toward stress. To prevent stress when already in a gloomy mood it may help to avoid social media that can trigger more negative emotions than positive. Stepping away from other people’s problems and reflecting on what is important. Meditation or long walks may provide avenues for detoxing negative emotions.

## 12. Aquarius (January 20 – February 18)

The Aquarius is an independent soul that will find stress relief in many ways. It is important for the Aquarius to maintain a level of originality in everything they do, and stress relief is no exception. With their giving nature, volunteering may help to relieve stress and create a feeling of peace within themselves. Finding different ways to help others when they have spare time will decrease the detachment they often feel from the rest of humanity.
