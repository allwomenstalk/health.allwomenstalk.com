# 7 Bath Bombs That'll Help Improve Your Health ...

Skip the shower today. Hop in the tub and read a book in it instead. It'll help you wind down from your stressful week. Here are a few bath bombs that'll give you the most relaxing bath of your life:

## 1. DIY Lush Bath Bomb

Have you heard of Sex Bomb by LUSH? It only costs six dollars at the store, but it's much more fun to make it yourself! All you're going to do is mix together epsom salts, baking soda, corn starch, coconut oil, lavender oil, and jasmine oil. This bath bomb will help relieve pain, reduce muscle cramps, eliminate toxins, and ease your stress.

## 2. Galaxy Bath Bomb

You've heard about galaxy bath bombs before, because they're stunning! If you don't want to buy one, you can create it yourself! You're only going to need a few different colors of food coloring, lemon juice, salt, baking soda, and a container to mix it all together. When you're finished, you'll have a few different colored products that you should stack on top of each other inside of an easter egg or an egg poacher. Leave it in the freezer for an hour and then pop it in your bath!

## 3. DIY Emoji Bath Bombs

If you want your bath bombs to look cute before you toss them in the bath, then you should follow this tutorial. You're going to need glycerin soap and some food coloring. After you mix those items together and let it dry, you're going to use a knife to cut out the features you need for the emoji's face. Then you can work on creating the actual bath bomb that you'll be attaching those features to.

## 4. DIY Mini Bath Bombs

You don't have to create a large bath bomb that you'll waste in one night. Instead, you can create these miniature ones with baking soda, lemon juice, sea salt, and vanilla extract. Pop them into a little mold, so you can create a dozen at once! If there's a holiday coming up, you can even wrap them up for your friends and family members!

## 5. Simple Bath Bombs

This is a basic bath bomb for beginners. You're going to put baking soda, citric acid, corn flour, and epsom salts into a large bowl. Whisk it together and then start combining your wet ingredients in a separate bowl (water, whatever oils you'd like, and whatever color food dye you'd like). Then you can combine everything and place it in your mold!

## 6. Lush Golden Egg Bath Bomb

You're going to start by creating a basic bath bomb, like you've seen in the tutorial above. After leaving it to dry for one to two days, you're going to remove it from your mold and rub melted coconut oil over it. Then you'll finish it off by covering it with glitter!

## 7. DIY Starbucks Bath Bomb

Do you want to create the cutest bath bomb that's ever existed? Then watch this tutorial. It'll show you how to create a bath bomb that looks like a Starbucks cup. If you're a fan of the store, then you're going to get a kick out of this procedure.

Now you know how to create DIY bath bombs that'll make your baths more relaxing than ever. Which one of these bath bombs are you going to create first?
