# 13 Daily Habits Most Women Have That Destroy Their Health and Beauty ...

**Daily Habits**-that’s a phrase that can seem harmless enough, right? But, oh boy, let me tell you, it's those innocent little things you do every single day that can sneakily become your own worst enemy. Especially for us women, as we juggle work, family, and trying to have some semblance of a social life, we might be picking up some habits that are doing more harm than good. Trust me, I know firsthand how easy it is to slip into certain routines without even realizing it. And since it's 2024, there's no better time to take stock and start anew!

So, let's dive into the world of **health** and _beauty_. Yes, those are big words, but don’t worry, I won’t throw around medical jargon or preachy advice. We're just having a friendly chat about those little daily _practices_ many of us are guilty of, and here’s a spoiler alert: A lot of it starts with your **morning routine**. Ever hit that snooze button a gazillion times? Yep, been there, done that. We’ll explore how this habit can mess with your natural **beauty sleep** and make you cranky all day. Check out more on this in "[The Snooze Trap](#1)".

Now, let’s talk about **nutrition**. I get it, life gets busy, and grabbing that quick snack or skipping meals can seem like the easiest option. But, spoiler alert, it’s sabotaging your health. You ever notice how a poorly timed sugar rush can wreak havoc on your skin? We’ll dive into how making some tweaks in your _diet_ can be a game-changer. You might want to read further in "[Snack Attacks: Friend or Foe?](#2)".

Speaking of snacks, let’s not forget about **hydration**. You'd be surprised at what just a little bit of dehydration can do to your cognitive function and skin elasticity. So, grab that cup of herbal tea, sit back, and let’s chat about making some small changes with big impacts. Because your _well-being_ deserves a little TLC, and it's never too late to start.

## 1. USING ONE TAMPON or PAD ALL DAY LONG

Pads or tampons should be changed every few hours. An old tampon or pad can cause an unpleasant vaginal infection and redness. Yikes!

## 2. WASHing YOUR BRA ONCE a WEEK

Experts advise that each bra should be washed after the third time we wear it.

## 3. Wearing a BLACK BRA in the SUMMER

Black bra in the summer is not a good idea. You don’t need a black bra to warm you in the summer and make you all sweaty. Just avoid them.

## 4. WEARING a BRA to SLEEP

This habit is very harmful. The bra presses your chest at night, and this interferes with the circulation and damages the breast tissue. So not good for your "girls"!

## 5. WASHING YOUR INTIMATE PARTS with SOAP

The vagina has its own cleaning mechanism, so soaps and shampoos are unnecessary. Just water is enough.

## 6. APPLYING ANTIPERSPIRANT DIRECTLY to YOUR SKIN

This is dangerous, it can irritate the skin and even cause breast cancer. It's best to apply it on your clothes.

## 7. GOING out in the SUN without PROTECTION

It's very important to protect your skin from the sun rays, especially in the summer. Always apply protective cream on your face and hands.

## 8. Not REMOVing MAKE-up before BED

I know removing makeup takes a little time, but it's worth it. Because sleeping with make up can cause premature aging and skin problems. Who wants those?

## 9. Not VISITing YOUR GYNECOLOGIST

Your intimate parts need a special care. It's important to be aware of the health of your vagina in order to avoid unwanted problems.

## 10. SKIPPING BREAKFAST

Diets again? That doesn’t mean you shouldn’t make time for it. Skipping breakfast won’t make you lose weight, it will only hurt your metabolism. You will feel tired and this may effect your mood, too. Eat!

## 11. BITING NAILS

This habit is not easy to get rid of. Very often people bite their nails as a daily habit, induced by stress or boredom. This poor habit not only ruins your manicure, but also allows the bacteria to enter your body.

## 12. EATING Too FAST without Chewing

Many of us forget to eat in a relaxed manner. Very often we are in a hurry for something, so the meal takes us 5 minutes. Each meal should last at least 20 minutes. Swallowing food without chewing can only cause stomachache, gassiness and bloating.

## 13. ALWAYS ASKing for PERMISSION

Many women are asking for permission every day, whether it’s a boss or a partner. Girls need to get rid of this habit as soon as possible! Be your own “Master” and don’t waste your time.
