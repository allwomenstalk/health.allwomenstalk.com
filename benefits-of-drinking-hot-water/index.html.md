# 13 Benefits of Drinking Hot Water ...

**Drinking hot water** is like giving your body a warm hug from the inside out. I mean, who wouldn’t want that, right? It’s amazing how something as simple as water, and hot water at that, can have such profound benefits for our well-being. And trust me, in **2024** where wellness trends are popping up faster than you can say "kombucha," this age-old practice remains as relevant and beneficial as ever.

First off, let’s get the obvious out of the way: if you’re someone like me, who’s always cold (and I mean always – even in the summer), a nice cup of hot water can be a literal life saver. But the benefits go way beyond just feeling toasty. For instance, did you know that starting your day with a glass of hot water can [aid digestion](#4)? It’s like giving your tummy a gentle wake-up call, saying "Hey, it’s time to get to work!"

Another incredible benefit is that **drinking hot water** can help [relieve nasal congestion](#7). Ever tried breathing through a blocked nose? It’s like trying to suck air through a really tight straw. Not fun. But a few sips of hot water can work miracles by loosening the mucus and making your breathing a whole lot easier.

Talking about miracles, another personal favorite is its ability to [improve circulation](#5). You see, I spend hours hunched over my desk writing articles (like this one), and sometimes I can almost feel the blood in my legs turn to molasses. But a warm cup of water revives me, encouraging better blood flow and reducing that "pins and needles" sensation.

Oh, and did I mention it helps with weight loss? Yeah, if you think it’s just another myth, think again! Drinking hot water can enhance your body's ability to [burn calories](#6) by warming it up from the inside, which helps improve metabolism. It's not a magic wand, but every bit helps, right?

Not to go all commercial on you, but trust me, the benefits don’t stop there. Reduced stress, healthy skin, detoxification-you name it, hot water’s got it covered. The secret sauce here is that hot water improves your overall **hydration**, which in turn enhances virtually every bodily function.

In my university days, I stumbled upon this practice quite by accident. Fast forward to today, and it’s a part of my daily routine. So, whether you’re a curious newcomer or a seasoned sipper, join me as we dive into the incredible [13 benefits of drinking hot water](#8). Grab your favorite mug, and let’s get started!

## 1. Better Digestion

Warm water in your stomach can help to stimulate better digestion, so your body won’t have to use so much energy to break down the food you are eating.

## 2. Detox

Hot water helps to flush out more toxins as you urinate and sweat. Your body temperature will rise as you drink it, and you will in turn sweat more.

## 3. Circulation

Hot water, in particular, is good at flushing out fat deposits, and doing this can greatly improve your blood circulation.

## 4. Pain Management

Drinking hot water is an easy and effortless way for women to ease their period cramps. The heat relaxes muscles in that area and relieves the pain.

## 5. Weight Loss

The more water you drink, the more hydrated you are, and this, in turn, increases your metabolism. A fast metabolism gives you a better chance of losing weight effectively.

## 6. Anti Ageing

Hot water helps to unclog pores, which in turn will increase elasticity in the face and also help to repairs cells quicker, giving an overall anti-aging effect.

## 7. Constipation

Constipation is often a result of dehydration, so the more hot water you drink, the more regular your bowel movements are going to be. The hot nature of it over cold water will also help to break down food faster.

## 8. Cleanse

Hot water is particularly good for cleansing your skin because the high temperatures open up your pores and allow the toxins to be flushed out.

## 9. Better Hair

Hot water can activate the nerves at the roots of your hair, helping to make them soft, and this will, in turn, encourage your hair to grow faster and stronger.

## 10. No Dandruff

The more hydrated your skin and scalp are, the less likely you are to have to deal with pesky dandruff on a regular basis.

## 11. Less Infection Risk

If you are travelling or camping, it’s always best to drink boiled water because the heating process will kill any bacteria and microbes that might be present.

## 12. Metabolism

We’ve covered it above, but it’s worth repeating again. Two cups of hot water a day can increase your metabolism by about 30 percent, which is a crazy amount for something so simple!

## 13. Sore Throat

Sometimes sore throats can be unbearable, and an immediate way to ease the pain is to sip on a hot cup of water. The heat will dissolve the thick viscous mucous membranes that are causing the problems.
