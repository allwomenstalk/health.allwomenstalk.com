# The Jaw Dropping Benefits of Thalassotherapy ...

Maybe you've never heard about the **benefits of thalassotherapy**. Maybe you don't even know what it is. You're not alone. The treatment is essentially a therapeutic bath and it loads of perks. You're going to love everything about it!

So let’s discover more about the **benefits of thalassotherapy!**

## 1. What is Thalassotherapy?

Thalassotherapy refers to a therapeutic bath that uses warm seawater. It's believed that this can boost circulation and remove toxins from your body. Thalassotherapy is uses to treat various medical conditions, including asthma, arthritis, obesity and cellulite. It's also great for relieving stress and easing tension in your muscles, which is one of the best **benefits of thalassotherapy.**

## 2. What's in the Water?

Thalassotherapy mainly involves marine minerals and sea water. This can be done with a bath, shower, mud bath or algae treatment. Spas offer thalassotherapy in different ways so find one that does it the way you want for the best results.

## 3. Thalassotherapy Treatment

Before choosing Thalassotherapy, you need to discuss the treatment with your doctor about any medical conditions your have, health problems and the desired outcome. You'll also want to mention this to your technician when you arrive at the spa. Typically, the treatment involves deep immersion into the warm sea water combined with gentle massage from pressurized jets. The procedure is usually followed with a massage, salt scrubbing, seaweed wrapping or a facial mudpack. Usually, having a short nap is suggested before leaving the spa.
