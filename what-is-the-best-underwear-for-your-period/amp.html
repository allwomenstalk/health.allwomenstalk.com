Frequently Asked Questions

What types of underwear are best for periods?
Period underwear with built-in absorbency or high-waist cotton briefs that offer comfort and leak protection are ideal.
Can I use regular underwear during my period?
You can, but pairing regular underwear with pads or tampons might not offer the same comfort or leak security as period-specific options.
Is period underwear reusable?
Yes, most period underwear is washable and reusable, making it an eco-friendly choice.
How often should I change my period underwear?
It's best to change them every 6 to 8 hours or as needed to stay fresh and prevent leaks.
Are there different absorbency levels in period underwear?
Yes, period underwear comes in various absorbencies ranging from light spotting to heavy flow.
Can period underwear replace pads or tampons?
Yes, period underwear is designed to replace or complement pads and tampons for many women.
Is period underwear comfortable for sensitive skin?
Many are made with soft, breathable fabrics that are gentle on sensitive skin.
How do I wash period underwear properly?
Rinse them in cold water, avoid fabric softeners, and air dry to maintain absorbency and fabric quality.
Can I use period underwear overnight?
Definitely, high-absorbency period underwear is great for overnight protection.
Are period panties discreet under clothing?
Yes, they are designed to look and feel like regular underwear without visible lines or bulk.
