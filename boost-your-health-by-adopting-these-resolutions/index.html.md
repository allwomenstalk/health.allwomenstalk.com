# Boost Your Health by Adopting These Resolutions ...

With the New Year comes new resolutions, right? It's a time of getting a fresh start and making over your lifestyle so that it can be as healthy and fabulous as possible. Wondering what kinds of things you can be doing this year? Here's a great list of **New Year's health resolutions **that will help you look and feel great in 2018.

## 1. Make Time for Exercise

![human action, fitness professional, sports, physical fitness, muscle,](https://img.allw.mn/content/p0/wz/jwbu5ahf5864b55b9354b541464621_400x225.gif)

If you haven't started making exercise a regular part of your routine, now is the time. Exercise wards of weight gain and helps prevent a whole bunch of health issues. Aim for 30 minutes each day.

## 2. Quit Smoking

![hair, person, blond, screenshot, Don't,](https://img.allw.mn/content/kv/vy/kn6dyzb55864b6a544104874225737_500x282.gif)

You probably know by now that smoking can kill you. Make this the year you talk to your doctor and take steps to quit, once and for all.

## 3. Make Time to Volunteer

![face, hair, person, woman, black hair,](https://img.allw.mn/content/yz/sr/az49cac05864b6e601cb6721641945_500x250.gif)

Of course, volunteering helps those you work for, but studies show that it can also improve your mood and outlook on life. It just feels good to help, so start today.

## 4. Give Yourself a Chance to Splurge

![screenshot,](https://img.allw.mn/content/jt/ag/bdf3prf25864b7ce56b6a896039657_400x225.gif)

Research shows that allowing yourself a splurge now and then reduces the risk of a total pig out. So enjoy one brownie and then get back on track without the guilt.

## 5. Start Cooking for Yourself

![meal, room, ill, lie,](https://img.allw.mn/content/mw/o7/ofcsd23u5864b80d5e256876260277_500x281.gif)

There's no doubt that preparing your own meals is healthier. You can control the ingredients and choose items that target your specific health goals this year.

## 6. Reduce Stress

![weather, snow, mouth, It's, hard,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ba/ni/nwt7wip85864b8feeb11c079095340_500x263.gif)

This is easier said than done, I know. Use relaxation techniques to help you cut stress, which improves mood and health.

## 7. Skip the Latest Fad Diets

![facial hair, Just, exercise, more, and,](https://img.allw.mn/content/un/f3/pcpzbt7z5864b9f3edaa5338916534_323x233.gif)

Instead of jumping on the bandwagon of the latest diet craze, go with the proven method of eating better and getting more exercise.

## 8. Sleep More

![human action, person, mouth, leg, interaction,](https://img.allw.mn/content/cb/d6/taq9xx065864babe4700e640829900_480x266.gif)

Skimping on sleep can affect your weight, mood and overall health, so make sure that 2018 is the year you start getting the recommended seven to nine hours every night.

## 9. Forget the Gym if You Hate It

![people, mud, walking, aig, BERRYHEA,](https://img.allw.mn/content/u2/wv/m2r70l105864bafa66ada791485761_500x281.gif)

There's no rule that says going to the gym is the only way to get in shape. Running in your neighborhood, going on a hike or bike ride or taking a dance class with your bestie all work too.

## 10. Give Yourself a Tech Break

![MOTE,](https://img.allw.mn/content/hl/bp/m24hl8pt5864bba825564981947553_500x209.gif)

Staring at a screen can impact your weight and it's not good for your neck, eyes or joints either. Schedule a block of time each day to unplug and 2018 will be your best year ever.

## 11. Plan a Trip

![organ, hand,](https://img.allw.mn/content/kf/dx/f9r9ngbt5864bc146f21d931082895_500x205.gif)

Travel gives you the chance to refresh and catch your breath. It's a great way to destress too. Where will you go in 2018?

## 12. Get Rid of Stuff

![,](https://img.allw.mn/content/se/ka/rorrdqvp5864bcc2cda81832466052_500x282.gif)

Being surrounded by clutter can really do a number on your mood and stress levels, which in turn can negatively impact your health. Resolve to clear out the junk this year.

## 13. Overhaul Your Finances

![person, art, speech, aMCI, GIFULMINATION.COM,](https://img.allw.mn/content/z3/h3/ypgl57bd5864be90bec4e022014269_640x360.gif)

Money troubles are stressful and can eat away at your general well being. Make this the year you get things under control for the sake of your health.

## 14. Cut Back on Drinking

![photography, beauty, fashion, model, PLL,](https://img.allw.mn/content/cg/d1/yut6lr7b5864bf44803e6806034664_500x226.gif)

Not water though! Cut back on the booze this year and you slash your risk of weight gain and cancer. Plus, you'll be more clear headed and happy too.

## 15. Boost Your Calcium Intake

![sense,](https://img.allw.mn/content/vk/k1/hfhestmb5864bfa971c5e829813709_497x254.gif)

Your bones need calcium to stay strong and healthy. Bone density starts to wane as you get older so keep your skeleton in tip-top shape by drinking more milk and eating cheese, yogurt and leafy green veggies.

## 16. Lose Those Stubborn Pounds

![person, people, crowd, audience, interaction,](https://img.allw.mn/content/gk/au/qr3whe945864c02d23df8565912461_500x247.gif)

Whether you have an extra 5 or 50 pounds hanging on, dropping them is important in 2018. Carrying around to much weight leads to heart disease, joint issues, diabetes and cancer.

## 17. Get Your Health Checked

![person, man, male, muscle, Weight?,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ob/ho/c579t38i5864c137b5080876653529_320x225.gif)

No matter your age, there are some important tests that can help your doctor assess your health. Examples include a breast and pelvic exam, a pap smear, a cholesterol screening, a mole check and a basic blood panel.

What's your health resolution this year?
