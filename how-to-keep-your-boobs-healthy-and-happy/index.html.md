# How to Keep Your Boobs Happy and Healthy ...

Your boobs are a beautiful part of your body. You don't want to do anything to damage them, which is why you should learn as much as you can about breast health. The more knowledge you have, the easier it'll be for you to keep your girls healthy and hopefully cancer free. If you're not sure what you should be doing to take care of your breasts, here are a few different ways to keep them happy and healthy:

## 1. Wear the Right Size Bra

Make sure you buy bras that actually fit you. You don't want to wear anything that's too tight against your skin. You should also avoid wearing a bra to bed. When you slip into your pajamas, slip out of your bra in order to give your breasts some room to breathe.

## 2. Limit Your Alcohol Intake

Alcohol and cigarettes can cause problems in dozens of different areas of your body. That's why you should quit smoking as soon as you can, and you should cut down on your alcohol consumption. It's also beneficial to cut down on your caffeine consumption, because it could cause problems, as well.

## 3. Eat Kale

There are certain foods that can play a role in helping you prevent breast cancer, such as broccoli, fish, apples, and garlic. It's also a great idea to eat kale, because it is low in fat and high in fiber. If you stick to a healthy diet by eating these foods, then not only will your breasts remain healthy, but the rest of your body will, as well.

## 4. Get Regular Exams

Women in their 20s and 30s should have a clinical breast exam at least once every three years. When you hit your 40s, then you should schedule a clinical breast exam and a mammogram every single year. That way, the doctors will be able to check to make sure your breasts are as healthy as they should be.

## 5. Exercise Often

You'll be healthier as a whole if you exercise. That's why you should head to the gym weekly, eat a healthy diet, and try your best to remain fit. Studies have shown that active women have a lower chance of getting breast cancer, which is why you should always aim to exercise for at least 150 minutes each week.

## 6. Breastfeed Your Children

If you ever have a child, make sure to breastfeed them. Not only is the milk healthy for the baby, but it's incredibly healthy for your chest. That's why you should breastfeed for as long as you can.

## 7. Learn the Signs of Cancer

The doctor shouldn't be the only one who checks you for cancer. You should know your own body enough to realize when there's a difference in your breasts. If there's an odd lump or weird discharge from your nipples, then something is wrong. Of course, the change might not always be visible, which is why you should give yourself a breast exam with your own hands once per month. If you're not sure how to, check out [nationalbreastcancer.org](https://www.nationalbreastcancer.org/breast-self-exam) for instructions.

You don't want to harm your breasts in any way, which is why you should follow these simple tips to keep them healthy and happy. What else do you do to keep your breasts healthy?
