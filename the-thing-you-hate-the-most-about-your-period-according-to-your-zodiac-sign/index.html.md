# The Thing You Hate the Most about Your Period According to Your Zodiac Sign ...

Here’s the thing, if you are woman who has periods on a regular basis, then I am sure you are one of many millions around the world who absolutely hate them! Sure, they are a natural part of being a biological woman, and there isn’t much you can do to avoid them, but that doesn’t mean that we shouldn’t be allowed to complain about them whenever we want! If guys can cry about man flu, then we can definitely moan about menstruation! Here is **the thing you hate most about your period according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

To put it bluntly, you hate all the mess! Although not that much of menstrual fluid truly is blood, you still hate the thought of it coming out of your body with such free will every single month!

## 2. Taurus

You hate the bloating that often occurs as a result of all the internal mechanics, especially when your period falls on a day when you were planning to do something special!

## 3. Gemini

You hate the fact that as soon as you start menstruating, your body starts demanding extra snacks of all varieties. Not only do you have to contend with things like cramps, you also have to fight off cravings for days!

## 4. Cancer

You hate the sore boobs that you always get when menstruating because they pose the risk of putting you in an even worse mood than the cramping.

## 5. Leo

You absolutely hate it when you are treated to a surprise early or late arrival. You are a creature of habit so no being able to predict your period drives you crazy.

## 6. Virgo

You are a materialistic person who loves to enjoy the finer things in life, which means that you hate whenever you have to deal with unwanted stains on clothing or bedding.

## 7. Libra

You never quite seem to be able to get yourself together when it comes to periods, and you hate the fact that are always caught short needing to borrow a tampon from someone!

## 8. Scorpio

You have enough to deal with on the front side of things, so you really hate it when your insides start playing up and you get those unwanted and too frequent bowel movements at the same time!

## 9. Sagittarius

You hate pain, plain and simple, so the thing about periods that you hate the most is most definitely the cramping.

## 10. Capricorn

You are a naturally happy and light person, so you hate the fact that your period has to make you think twice about laughing heartily for fear of a leak!

## 11. Aquarius

You just hate the combination of everything above, which translates, as we all know, into the dreaded second day of a period when it seems like the whole world is against you!

## 12. Pisces

You are a very physical and intimate person, so you hate the way that your period disrupts the natural and spontaneous flow of your sex life.
