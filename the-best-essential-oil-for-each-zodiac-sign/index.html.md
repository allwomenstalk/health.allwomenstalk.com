# The Best Essential Oil for Each Zodiac Sign ...

Want to know **the best essential oil for each zodiac sign**?
Regardless of whether you are into astrology or not, there is no denying that essential oils hold a lot of power. You can digest them and you can use them in a more traditional way as a mood booster to help cope with stress, and the best part about them is that there are so many different kinds to choose from. The choice can be overwhelming for somebody who doesn’t know where to start, so let’s indulge in a little star searching to help you out! Here is **the best essential oil for each zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aquarius

You love nothing more than a fresh idea or experience, so the fresh nature of peppermint is the perfect match!

## 2. Pisces

You are a gentle soul who loves nothing better than to have some quiet time to reconnect with yourself. Tea tree oil is perfect for this, with its healing and cleansing properties.

## 3. Aries

Being a natural born leader can often put you under great stress, so nothing will be better for you than classic, traditional soothing lavender.

## 4. Taurus

You are a practical person who likes to stay busy, which means that you need to keep your immune system as boosted as possible. Lemon is your go-to oil, without a doubt.

## 5. Gemini

You are prone to a touch of fatigue due to your fast-paced nature, so the uplifting and energizing power of May Chang is a perfect choice.

## 6. Cancer

You like nothing more than to feel safe and secure in your surroundings, and geranium oil is well known for making people feel grounded and relaxed in their environment.

## 7. Leo

You have so much energy that you sometimes can’t switch off at night, but Clary Sage can be very helpful with this problem, easing you into a good night’s sleep.

## 8. Virgo

Your perfectionist nature means that you always strive to be on top form, so orange essential oil will help to keep your health in check.

## 9. Libra

You have a tendency to be more tightly wound than the rest of the star signs, so you need something that is going to help you to relax. Frankincense is perfect for this, as it has the power to relieve anxiety and stress.

## 10. Scorpio

You are bold and passionate, so you need something that will help to balance your mood out in calmer times, and rose essential oil is the thing to do this.

## 11. Sagittarius

You have a tendency to sometimes want to overdo things thanks to your natural restlessness. Ginger helps to improve circulation to all of your limbs, which might help you to feel less antsy.

## 12. Capricorn

You like to always be on the go which might be good for your mind but tiring for your body. Rosemary essential oil is really good for your joints and your skin, helping you on the inside and the outside.
