# 24 Simple Ways to Incorporate Mindfulness into Your Daily Life ...

it's funny that while we live in a self-centric and even selfish world, that many of us do not practice mindfulness. Many of us live a shallow life, not making the most of the world around us, its bounty, our advantages and our opportunities. I think many of us would admit we don't make the most of every moment, that we aren't present in every challenge and every triumph. Being more mindful is a goal that is achievable for every one of us and a goal we're totally in control of. Being mindful can be practised every moment of every day - you just need to form the habit. Here's a whole pile of ways to be more mindful every day. Thank you mindbodygreen.com

## 1. Stretch First Thing in the Morning

![pink, woman, flower, beauty, plant,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ot/pr/s6kgo2e4591df5bc3eee6548329761_600x750.jpg)

Doing some stretching and breathing right after you wake up in the morning, will energize you and center you for the day ahead.

## 2. Drink Water All Day

![sign,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g4/pq/zj5t6zdz591df77eb5650659387252_600x600.jpg)

Drink lots of water during the day, starting with a glass at breakfast. Your body dehydrates overnight, so refilling the tank early will help you be more present and energetic throughout the day.

## 3. Chew Your Food Slowly

![dish, meal, food, eating,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sl/fy/ze4heu26591df5e31080b331115563_600x749.jpg)

And enjoy the texture and the taste! You’ll be more satisfied with less food and more likely to notice other subtleties throughout the day.

## 4. Write down Your Top Three Priorities for the Day, First Thing in the Morning

![handwriting, writing, brand, document,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gw/bj/b7kd3hnh591df5fed0afe697378002_600x600.jpg)

Especially before you check your email. You’ll be a lot less likely to get caught up in mindlessly reacting to whatever hits your screen during any given day.

## 5. Listen to Music

![hair, hairstyle, glasses, selfie, hairdresser,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tv/ry/ru1gg64i591df663b6cd3720421434_600x600.jpg)

If you listen to music that moves you instead of mindless chatter from the flat screen, you will in turn make a conscious choice to listen to things that nourish you, rather than deplete your mind and heart.

## 6. Journal

![text, writing, font, diagram, document,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rm/gh/wpxyt3xj591df6ab3fd67004217530_600x600.jpg)

Write in your journal to capture your reflections on your purpose and how you’re continuing to show up for that. Over time, you’ll create a record of your life that will provide valuable perspective.

## 7. Ask Yourself, “What’s Working Right Now?”

![lady, leg, beauty, thigh, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ek/df/vbm5505c591df6d705817536953704_600x600.jpg)

No matter how bad things seem, there is almost always something that’s going right. Mindfully build on that.

## 8. Breathe Deeply

![hair, clothing, black hair, thigh, undergarment,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cw/ph/rw8gsw6c591df716e9232658740453_600x600.jpg)

If you take three deep breaths from your belly before and after every conversation, you'll be more present during the conversation and clear your mind after.

## 9. Put Your Phone in Airplane Mode when You’re in Meetings

![hair, clothing, sitting, human positions, lady,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u3/xb/b4865jkf591df7351c721541038331_600x749.jpg)

Freeing yourself from the distractions of your smartphone will remove a lot of the interruptions that can keep you from being fully present.

## 10. Slow down!

![clothing, footwear, beauty, fashion, leg,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y5/h4/cpe6xy29591df74fb52f2340131254_600x750.jpg)

If you take a moment to stop and smell the roses, you'll have more appreciation for all the beauty and abundance that life has to offer.

## 11. Practice "transformational Listening."

![hair, beauty, eating, meal, sense,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/af/e4/goictktu591df82cdcbc6702926623_600x749.jpg)

Listening fully and without an agenda builds the relationships that make life sweet, as opposed to "transactional" or "transient" listening.

## 12. Stretch and Move Often

![clothing, active undergarment, muscle, arm, undergarment,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/do/c1/z3qcebhf591df821f0e7b975067819_600x600.jpg)

There's plenty of research that shows that “sitting is the new smoking.” Get out of your chair regularly for at least five minutes every hour to optimize your health and well-being.

## 13. Go out for Lunch

![eating, food, cuisine, glasses,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mx/wq/xgnc9ocb591df859e680a932202127_600x750.jpg)

Take lunch away from your desk. Your body and brain deserve the break!

## 14. Get outside!

![clothing, dress, lady, pink, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m7/fy/sejkwj3i591df892d552e497161489_600x749.jpg)

No matter what the weather's like, learn to appreciate the now by enjoying the beauty of the outdoors, regardless of your scenery.

## 15. Tell Someone How Much You Appreciate Them

![clothing, image, beauty, sun tanning, vacation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uc/wc/cb18e70u591df87832c63866098437_600x749.jpg)

Be specific - it may be the first time anyone has ever shared that compliment with them. What a gift!

## 16. Notice What Time Frame Your Mind is in

![clothing, dress, lady, pink, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pj/m8/ig0t6i0u591df8b27836a418316290_600x749.jpg)

If it’s in the past or future, gently bring it back to the present.

## 17. Eat Fresh, Whole Foods

![meal, flower arranging, floristry, flower, christmas decoration,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mv/ps/hk12az77591df8cc603d4960174716_600x659.jpg)

Snacks like nuts, veggies or low-sugar fruit are much better for you than processed foods, and will curb hunger, giving you more energy and focus throughout the day.

## 18. Question Your Assumptions

![hair, face, black hair, eyebrow, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wi/u2/dj7wjyot591df8f81bfae962648190_600x600.jpg)

Expand and challenge the way you look at people and situations.

## 19. Call an Old Friend

![clothing, beauty, blond, fashion, supermodel,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zz/z9/whtztsks591df919bc09e478706362_600x522.jpg)

It will warm their heart and yours!

## 20. Move Your Body in a Mindful Way for at Least 30 Minutes

![human action, sports, outdoor recreation, tights, recreation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mp/fi/o4srj0sm591df94df33fe375470771_600x600.jpg)

Think of it as a moving meditation.

## 21. Put Your Phone in a Drawer for at Least a Couple of Hours when You Get Home Each Evening

![human action, hair, person, human positions, sitting,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ee/vj/dmoxwrib591df966a51c8348859337_500x616.jpg)

You may be surprised and delighted by what you notice.

## 22. Write a Personal Note (on Paper) to Say “Thanks!” or “I Love You” (or Both!)

![pink, flower, purple, plant, petal,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k7/l9/h66wdkzr591df9779efc6389506007_600x600.jpg)

There’s a good chance that person will cherish it forever.

## 23. Take Time Each to Day to Reflect on Why You’re Here

![clothing, image, human positions, swimwear, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yl/mp/xvygo19z591df98684356553605289_600x707.jpg)

Celebrate one thing you did today that served that very purpose.

## 24. Get a Good Night’s Sleep

![hair, human action, clothing, beauty, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hx/iy/gkecsmq0591df9b49d489023975499_600x721.jpg)

Keep your smartphone and tablet out of your bedroom. The light from them messes up your sleep pattern. You need a good night’s rest for a great day tomorrow!
