# Feeling Stressed? These Relaxing GIFs Will Calm You down ...

When you're feeling stressed, there are plenty of ways to calm yourself down. One of the simplest cures is to look at some relaxing images that'll make your worries drift away. Of course, there's only one thing better than looking at images, and that's looking at moving images, so here are some of the most relaxing GIFs that are sure to calm you down:

## 1. Waterfall Wonders

![habitat,natural environment,atmospheric phenomenon,ecosystem,forest,](https://img.allw.mn/content/dp/e1/qjlth_habitat_natural-environment_atmospheric-phenomenon_ecosystem_forest_768x368.gif)

Even though this GIF is a drawing and not an actual picture, it's just as relaxing as looking at a real waterfall would be. If you love nature, but aren't in the mood to go outdoors, GIFs like this are all you need to calm yourself down.

## 2. Pretty Kitty

![cartoon,logo,font,illustration,fictional character,](https://img.allw.mn/content/fn/m5/gt747_cartoon_logo_font_illustration_fictional-character_500x250.gif)

It's impossible to stop yourself from smiling when you look at this pretty kitty. I mean, look how adorable he is! Bop your head along with him and you'll be in a better mood in no time.

## 3. Special Snowfall

![phenomenon,weather,atmosphere,midnight,snow,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ub/ic/nvup6_phenomenon_weather_atmosphere_midnight_snow_500x255.gif)

Maybe you've never seen snow, or maybe you're sick of it and wish the Winter would end already. Either way, you can't deny the beauty of this GIF. Nature is just plain amazing.

## 4. Addictive Drawing

![white,floor,flooring,material,drawing,](https://img.allw.mn/content/xs/yz/q5590_white_floor_flooring_material_drawing_500x260.gif)

Something as simple as following the stroke of this paintbrush can relax you. Why? Because it's such a gentle, beautiful movement.

## 5. Perfect Palms

![tree,morning,arecales,sunlight,wind,](https://img.allw.mn/content/gd/zh/jfniv_tree_morning_arecales_sunlight_wind_500x281.gif)

If there's nothing special outside of your window, you can stare at this GIF instead. Pay attention to the movement of the trees and the drifting of the clouds.

## 6. Rainy Day

![green,leaf,window,texture,reflection,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ow/t0/hbtlo_green_leaf_window_texture_reflection_540x360.gif)

There's something relaxing about rain. The only thing better than watching it fall is sitting next to a kitty during the experience.

## 7. Coffee Cup

![man made object,cup,coffee cup,drink,caffeine,](https://img.allw.mn/content/aw/w1/cz1rb_man-made-object_cup_coffee-cup_drink_caffeine_400x300.gif)

Even if you can't stand the smell or taste of coffee, you should enjoy this GIF. The steam rising from the cup could hold your attention all day.

## 8. Rushing River

![habitat,natural environment,ecosystem,grass,river,](https://img.allw.mn/content/km/pa/py6vr_habitat_natural-environment_ecosystem_grass_river_500x269.gif)

Whether water is falling from the sky or rushing down a river, it's mesmerizing. If you've never considered yourself an outdoorsy person before this, that'll change soon.

## 9. Soaring through Skies

![sky,cloud,atmospheric phenomenon,atmosphere,weather,](https://img.allw.mn/content/sn/x3/wi29h_sky_cloud_atmospheric-phenomenon_atmosphere_weather_640x360.gif)

This GIF should help you see how small your problems are in the grand scheme of life. The world is too beautiful to stress about the little things.

## 10. Perfect Pool

![property,home,estate,swimming pool,resort,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ls/qu/qfu6x_property_home_estate_swimming-pool_resort_500x264.gif)

There's so much to focus on in this picture. You can look at the water, the land, or the mountains in the background. It's all equally beautiful.

## 11. Sleepy Kittens

![cat,mammal,vertebrate,nose,whiskers,](https://img.allw.mn/content/fu/zl/ltv8m_cat_mammal_vertebrate_nose_whiskers_480x264.gif)

Every creature looks beautiful while sleeping. If you don't have a pet to cuddle with, spend some time admiring this GIF.

## 12. Beneath the Bridge

![tunnel,light,darkness,infrastructure,](https://img.allw.mn/content/yo/bb/s5ojg_tunnel_light_darkness_infrastructure_500x211.gif)

You probably wouldn't want to be stuck in this storm, but it's pretty to look at. If nothing else, it'll help you appreciate the fact that you're warm, cozy, and dry.

## 13. City Snow

![black and white,weather,monochrome photography,monochrome,](https://img.allw.mn/content/gj/wo/sjp57_black-and-white_weather_monochrome-photography_monochrome_499x283.gif)

Here's some more snow for you. This angle captures the beauty of a Winter night in the city.

## 14. Can't Get Enough Coffee

![cup,coffee cup,drink,morning,saucer,](https://img.allw.mn/content/h6/e5/if2tx_cup_coffee-cup_drink_morning_saucer_500x281.gif)

Here's another cup of coffee to keep you relaxed. If you need to, go grab a cup of your own. It'll create maximum relaxation.

## 15. Wondrous Waves

![green,wave,wind wave,ocean,wind,](https://img.allw.mn/content/vg/vk/pk0y6_green_wave_wind-wave_ocean_wind_500x269.gif)

This GIF will have you staring all day, because waves are about as amazing as it gets. Nature will just keep surprising you with its beauty.

These GIFs should help you calm down, and help you realize how much beauty is in the world. What do you typically do in order to de-stress?
