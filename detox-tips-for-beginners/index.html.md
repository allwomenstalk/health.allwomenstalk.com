# 8 Detox Tips for Beginners ...

If you want to purify and remove toxins from your body, the following detox tips for beginners can help. A detox is an excellent way to boost your mental and physical health. Many illnesses, chronic fatigue and brain fog are caused by a buildup of toxins. These toxins are present in our foods and the air we breathe. Want to improve your health? Here are eight detox tips for beginners.

## 1. Increase Fiber Intake

Need a few detox tips for beginners? Eat plenty of fresh fruits, vegetables and whole grains such as brown rice to remove toxins from your body. A low-fiber diet plays a role in constipation, and when stools are irregular, toxins can accumulate in your digestive tract. Take a daily fiber supplement or eat at least five servings of fruits and vegetables. This reduces toxins and contributes to a healthy digestive tract.

## 2. Limit Sugar Intake

Too much sugar in your diet can have a toxic effect on your body. The more sugar you consume, the more insulin your body produces. This can strain your pancreas and trigger chronic fatigue. Plus, an out-of-control sugar habit increases your risk for diabetes and extra pounds.

## 3. Drink Eight Glasses of Water a Day

Skip sodas and sugary juices. Water keeps your body hydrated and helps flush excess sodium from your body. Additionally, water promotes healthy digestion and alleviates constipation. This facilitates the removal of waste from your body - thus the removal of toxins.

## 4. Get Active

Regular exercise stimulates blood circulation and encourages healthy bowel activity. What’s more, sweat releases toxins from your body. Make exercise a part of your everyday routine. Aim for a minimum of 30 minutes of activity at least five days a week.

## 5. Drink Green Tea

This beverage is loaded with antioxidants and helps repair cell damage. Drink a few cups of green tea daily to stay hydrated and flush toxins from your body. But these aren’t the only benefits of this beverage. Green tea is also a metabolism booster. This is great for weight loss.

## 6. Go Organic

Yes, organic foods are more expensive. But if you’re serious about detoxing your body, organic is the way to go. These foods are produced without the use of pesticides or other synthetic materials, making them a safer alternative. Shop at grocery stores that specialize in organic foods, or browse the organic section of your nearest grocery store.

## 7. Go Green

Toxins aren’t only in the foods we eat, but also the chemicals and products we use. Each time you disinfect your home or use personal hygiene products, you risk exposure to harmful environmental pollutants. Exposure may not cause immediate harm, but it can affect your health in the future. Consider eco-friendly household and personal hygiene products.

## 8. Sit in a Sauna

A sauna is one of the fastest and best ways to relax and detoxify your body. Saunas promote perspiration - and the more you sweat, the more toxins you release. Take a moment to unwind in the sauna after a workout, or spend a few hours at your local spa. Saunas not only release toxins, but promote weight loss.

The less toxins in your body, the better you’ll feel. Make your health a priority and take steps to rid your body of toxins that can contribute to sluggishness and diseases. When was your last detox? What is your favorite detox method?
