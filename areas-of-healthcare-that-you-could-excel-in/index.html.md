# 5 Areas of Healthcare That You Could Excel in ...

In the healthcare industry, there are many different areas for you to get involved in from nursing to pharmacy, healthcare marketing and much more. Many people believe that the healthcare industry is only for doctors or nurses but there are so many roles that are never considered.

Here, we will discuss some of the areas in healthcare that you could excel in. Be sure to keep reading for some more information.

## 1. Nursing

One of the most popular areas to get involved in when it comes to healthcare is nursing either working in hospitals, care homes or in other departments. Nursing is a popular area because there are many different things involved in the day to day tasks of a nurse. From treating patients to being present during surgeries and much more, nurses can be given many different tasks to carry out each day. If you would like to excel in nursing, you should make sure you get the right training as soon as possible.

## 2. Pharmacy

Another popular area to get involved in when it comes to the healthcare industry is pharmacy work. When working in a pharmacy you will need to be responsible for giving patients the correct [prescriptions](https://www.webmd.com/drugs/2/index) and treatments that have been advised to them by doctors. To excel in pharmacy, you will need to make sure you are organised and that you give the correct treatments and medicines to each patient.

## 3. Healthcare Marketing

One of the most interesting sectors in the healthcare industry is one that many people do not know about – healthcare marketing. This is interesting because it can allow you to see a different side of the healthcare industry with research and other tasks. If you would like to excel in healthcare marketing then, you should consider taking [healthcare marketing](https://dhge.org/our-programs/healthcare-marketing-certificate-pepperdine-graziadio/) course. You can do this online so make sure to consider if you want to try something new.

## 4. Surgery

Surgery is another area that many people try to excel in when it comes to the healthcare industry and this is because it can be a very challenging yet rewarding job. People require surgery for many reasons and sometimes it can be lifesaving or life-changing. To become a [surgeon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Surgeon), you will need to take on many years of training and so it is a very big commitment. If this is something that you would like to try out, make sure to find the right course for you today.

## 5. Change Your Career

Overall, there are many different areas involved in healthcare and some roles are not as popular as others. You don’t need to be a doctor or a nurse to help others, you can get involved in marketing or even become a pharmacist. Make sure that you keep this information in mind and use it to help you decide which area you would like to get involved in when it comes to healthcare. Now is your time to change your career!
