One of the best **health benefits of activated charcoal** is that it fights bloating. We have all been through days when we feel so bad that we vow never to drink again, but activated charcoal can help with even the worst hangovers! Having a stash of activated charcoal tablets at all times means that you will be able to clean any water that you might need to drink. There is no need to spend hundreds of dollars on teeth whitening procedures. All you need is some activated charcoal!
