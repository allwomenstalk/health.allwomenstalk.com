# Daily Health Tips for Girls Who Want a Better Life ...

These **daily health tips** are ideal for ensuring that every day of the week is just as great as it can be. Let's be honest, some days are just not ones you look forward to. Monday, I'm looking at you. But that doesn't have to be the case. By incorporating healthy habits into your week, every day will be one you look forward to. Here are some **daily health tips** that should have you loving every day.

## 1. Sunday

![beach, sea, physical fitness, sky, vacation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oq/ty/m3y4738h5a035cde45d57987679038_600x599.jpg)

The first of the **daily health tips** is to meditate on Sundays. By sitting quietly and focusing on you, you set yourself up for a great week to come. It only takes 5 to 10 minutes, but the payoffs are huge.

## 2. Monday

![fashion model, shoulder, outerwear, joint, photo shoot,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qe/lq/pgfbh7wv5a035dc644600225590449_600x600.jpg)

Monday isn't anyone's favorite day of the week, but if you can force yourself to get up an hour earlier, you'll start the week off right. You can get a load of things done in that extra hour, then enjoy a less stressful week.

## 3. Tuesday

![product, font,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iz/un/lzx74z545a035d57d6935607308843_600x600.jpg)

Tuesday is for updating your to-do list. There's something so satisfying about marking things off, so write your list in a way that makes it easy to do that. Your sense of accomplishment will improve every single day.

## 4. Wednesday

![active undergarment, shoulder, physical fitness, weight training, thigh,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ln/an/wba56n3e5a035e634240d648691899_600x600.jpg)

Get moving on Wednesday. Whether you hit the gym, take a walk on your lunch break or you just make a point of getting up and walking around the office building every hour or so, you'll reap the benefit of burning more calories and feeling more energized and happier.

## 5. Thursday

![font, brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v7/zk/e9tmvuh95a035f0888b3b741278734_600x600.jpg)

The week is almost over and today is a great day to have some chocolate to get you through. Chocolate tastes good and makes you happy, but research shows that it also helps boost cognition. What better reason to nibble a treat right now?

## 6. Friday

![fun, interaction, girl, product, recreation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c5/ne/o1h127oi5a035f3304734281945256_600x600.jpg)

Your natural inclination on Friday night might be to go out, but you might enjoy having a night in instead. Spend it with your significant other or a close friend. You'll love the chance to bond with someone special in your life and you won't have a nasty hangover in the morning.

## 7. Saturday

![active undergarment, fashion model, thigh, abdomen, lingerie,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ei/pb/af46539q5a035fafa1727598433096_600x600.jpg)

Everyone loves a day off, but don't waste the whole day in bed. Get up and go for a walk. You won't have any time pressures and you can enjoy a nearby trail. If you have a dog, take him with you.

Which day of the week is your favorite? How do you make the bad ones better?
