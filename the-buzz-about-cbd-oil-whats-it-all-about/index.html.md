# The Buzz about CBD Oil: Whats It All about ...

You have to have been living in a bubble to not notice the buzz about CBD oil. It is an oil that is extracted from the concentrated form of a cannabis plant, and over the last few years, there has been a huge wave of popularity in the usage of it for various medicines and products in lots of countries around the world. If you are not so familiar with it yet, then this is a good place to start. This buzz about CBD oil, what’s it all about? Here are just some of the **uses of CBD**.

## 1. Treating Epilepsy

There have been multiple studies at this point to prove that CBD oil can be extremely effective in the treatment of epilepsy, so much so that the FDA has deemed it an officially acceptable to approve it as a new drug to add to the rotation.

## 2. Pain Relief

One of the most prominent features of CBD oil is its pain relieving properties. The benefits that it can bring to people who suffer from things like arthritis and other joint pain are almost unrivalled in anything other than strong opioids, and for these reasons it is deemed as a very sensible and prescribable option for lots of people of all ages around the world.

## 3. Anxiety and Depression

Anxiety and depression are either on the rise or just more openly diagnosed in today’s society, and CBD oil has been identified as a leading treatment for reducing the symptoms of both. The chemical makeup of CBD oil has the power to relieve your body of much of the stress symptoms for short periods of time, and it has the benefit of being nowhere near as addictive as something like benzodiazepines that are causing a crisis in many parts of the world.

## 4. Cancer Symptoms

To be clear, CBD oil does not have the power to cure cancer, but what it has been identified as being able to do is reduce many of the side effects of the necessary treatment including nausea, vomiting, and general pain.

## 5. Acne

In the less serious side of the health scale, CBD oil can also be of great help to people who suffer from bad acne. It is a skin condition that affects nearly ten percent of the population and CBD oil’s anti-inflammatory properties work directly with your skin to reduce the amount of sebum that you produce, which in turn will decrease the risk of greasy and oily outbreaks.
