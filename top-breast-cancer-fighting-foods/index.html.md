# Top 7 Breast Cancer Fighting Foods ...

As a woman, you may wonder about breast cancer fighting foods. One way you can help fight against breast cancer is by eating the right foods. Some foods that fight cancer are packed with important nutrients that really help prevent or fight the disease. If you are interested in learning about cancer fighting foods, you are not going to want to miss this list.

## 1. Fatty Fish

Fatty fish like tuna and salmon contain two main nutrients that help against breast cancer. First, these fish are high in omega-3s. Omega-3s are known for decreasing tumor growth and boosting the immune system. These fish are also a great source of vitamin D, which has been shown to reduce the risk of breast cancer. Try to eat 2 to 3 servings of fish each week.

## 2. Green Tea

The amazing antioxidant in green tea makes it a perfect breast cancer fighting food. If you want to lower your risk of breast cancer and ovarian cancer, trade in your coffee for green tea. Studies show that 3 to 4 servings of green tea may decrease your risk of breast cancer. Green tea is also known to boost metabolism. Have you already added green tea to your diet? I like to drink mine warm with a little bit of honey.

## 3. CruciferousVeggies

Cruciferous vegetables are fantastic cancer fighting foods. Cruciferous veggies include broccoli, cauliflower, cabbage and kale. These are wonderfully nutritious veggies, packed with special compounds that make it hard for breast cancer cells to grow. For the best results, try to eat these cruciferous vegetables raw. Many of these veggies are great for dipping.

## 4. Beans

More and more studies are showing how beans and lentils can really help lower a person’s risk of cancer. They are loaded with all the right antioxidants and fibers to fight breast cancer. Try to add beans to your regular diet. They make a great addition to many recipes and side dishes, but they're also delicious and nutritious on their own.

## 5. Oatmeal

I’m sure by now you know how wonderful oatmeal is for your health. It is known to lower cholesterol, boost the immune system, and fight against diabetes. In case you needed one more reason to start your mornings off with a yummy bowl of oatmeal, studies now show oatmeal can be a key breast cancer fighting food. This is because diets rich in fiber coming from whole grains, such as oatmeal, protect against breast cancer.

## 6. Fruits

Just like the other breast cancer fighting foods, most fruits are filled with good antioxidants. Eat fruits that are rich in color, like strawberries, oranges, red grapes, and blueberries. Snack on them by themselves, eat them in salads, or work them into other recipes. Just make sure you eat plenty of them each day.

## 7. Walnuts

Walnuts are another breast cancer fighting food. They are a great snack to keep on hand and now we know they may help against breast cancer. Don’t hesitate to add walnuts to your regular diet. Studies show that for the best results, you should try to eat 2 ounces of walnuts every day.

I hope you take these breast cancer fighting foods into consideration. Most of these would be very simple to add into your daily diet. You can add fish to the menu a few times a week, trade red meat for beans, have oatmeal with walnuts and fruit for breakfast and snack on veggies throughout the day. It’s these simple changes that can give you the lead in your fight against breast cancer. What are some ways you plan to add these breast cancer fighting foods to your diet?
