# 7 Things You do on the Daily That Are Hurting Your Breasts ...

You love your body, which is why you should take good care of it. Sometimes, that's hard to do, because we don't know enough about how to treat our breasts. Thankfully, [Redbook Mag](https://www.redbookmag.com/body/g2936/reasons-why-boobs-sag/) has some advice for you. Here are some things you're probably doing wrong when it comes to protecting your breasts:

## 1. You Don't Drink Enough OJ

![You Don't Drink Enough OJ](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sr/yh/fqy88w0n5620ffc5dcfe8737115748_480x712.jpg)

Your breasts need vitamin C and B. It'll help them maintain their shape.

## 2. You Eat Chips, Candy, and Donuts

![You Eat Chips, Candy, and Donuts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wz/bs/zrwcj1x65620ffc691a4b010100655_480x646.jpg)

You don't want fat around your breasts. If you eat too much junk food, that could happen.

## 3. You Are Hitting Your Weight-loss Goals

![You Are Hitting Your Weight-loss Goals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ex/sm/aut3y23w5620ffd326067088036449_480x639.jpg)

Losing too much weight too quickly could have a negative impact on your breasts. It could cause them to sag.

## 4. You Smoke a Lot

![You Smoke a Lot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z3/sw/quo9izx75620ffc65cefd464276474_480x639.jpg)

Smoking can ruin the skin around your breasts. That means you need to quit the habit while you still can.

## 5. You Forget to Rub down the Twins

![You Forget to Rub down the Twins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p3/ns/el32a2tm5620ffc684c10726082941_480x721.jpg)

Use moisturizer to massage your breasts. It'll keep them hydrated.

## 6. You Go for Long Runs Every Day

![You Go for Long Runs Every Day](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tn/bi/nrxh7h825620ffc6ce8c5700823391_480x642.jpg)

Too much bouncing can cause your breasts to sag. That's why you might want to switch up your exercise routine.

## 7. You Don't Protect Them from the Sun

![You Don't Protect Them from the Sun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b5/y8/egygl36c5620ffc71acae987792720_480x636.jpg)

Even if your breasts are covered, you should still place sunscreen on them. It'll protect them from becoming dehydrated and damaged.
