# 7 Aromatherapy Products to Keep You Healthy and Happy ...

Aromatherapy products have a variety of purposes that can enhance your life. From natural cleaning products to fragrances, aromatherapy products come in many forms. Aromatherapy is the therapeutic use of essential oils that have been extracted from plants. Aromatherapy works by stimulating smell receptors in the nose, which then send messages through the nervous system to the limbic system - the area of the brain that controls emotions. Aromatherapy can enhance your mood, relieve stress, and improve your health. Find your new favorite aromatherapy product by exploring this list!

## 1. Clary Peace of Green

Stress-relieving Peace of Green is one of my go-to aromatherapy products, and is available at Clary Sage Salon & Spa. It helps relieve tension and raise vitality levels with the balancing aromas of a stimulating peppermint blend. In addition to relieving stress, Peace of Green can help relieve headaches, tension, and nasal allergy symptoms. I love using Peace of Green when I travel-it’s great for protecting you against germs in crowded airplane cabins. The convenient pocket-sized packaging allows you to always have Peace of Green with you for stress emergencies!

## 2. Dr. Bronner’s Lavender Liquid Soap

Lavender is an essential oil known for relaxing those who are tired and stressed out. It calms your nerves and soothes the body. The heavenly scent of lavender in Dr. Bronner’s Lavender Liquid Soap is amazing and creates a perfect atmosphere for a tranquil bath or shower. In addition, it’s certified organic and feels amazing on the skin.

## 3. Caldrea Laundry Detergent

Caldrea Laundry Detergent uses powerful plant-derived surfactants that will keep your whites bright and your colors vivid. It’s safe to use on all fabrics and works well with any water temperature. This laundry detergent comes in 8 different fragrances that provide an aromatherapeutic experience. The natural essential oils help remove stains in addition to making your laundry smell great!

## 4. DoTERRA on Guard Protective Essential Oil Blend

On Guard is an exclusive essential oil blend from doTERRA that is formulated to support healthy immune function. It contains wild orange, clove, cinnamon, eucalyptus, and rosemary essential oils that offer a natural and effective alternative to synthetic options for immune system support. If you feel a cold or flu coming on, apply some On Guard to your pulse points to help strengthen your body to fight off infection. Use it regularly to keep you healthy all the time!

## 5. Elemental Herbology Fire Zest Body Essentials

This aromatherapy product from Elemental Herbology helps fight jet lag and gives you a boost of energy! Massage in this oil that is infused with basil, tangerine, ylang ylang, and rosemary in order to increase your vitality while softening the skin. Help soothe acute and chronic fatigue naturally rather than pumping yourself full of caffeine!

## 6. Antica Farmacista Diffuser in Lavender and Lime Blossom

I love using home fragrance diffusers to freshen up my apartment. I love the unique combination of lavender and lime to calm the senses while still providing uplifting qualities. The pretty bottle makes for great room décor, and it comes packaged in a cream colored box, making it a pretty gift as well. The reed diffusers are made from white birch, which allows the scent to permeate the air to provide a lasting and constant scent.

## 7. Mrs. Meyer's Clean Day Countertop Spray

Mrs. Meyer's Clean Day Countertop Sprays feature a variety of essential oil scents to help you naturally clean your counters. The ingredients in this spray are at least 98% naturally derived, so you know it’s safe and effective. It works best on non-porous surfaces in the kitchen and bathroom. This product is affordable and is sold at most Target stores, so it’s easy to get your hands on!

Lift your spirits and improve your health by incorporating these aromatherapy products into your daily routine. Take the natural approach to enhance your happiness and health with essential oils and aromatherapy products. What aromatherapy products do you love?
