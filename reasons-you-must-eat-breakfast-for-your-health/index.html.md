# 7 Reasons You Must Eat Breakfast for Your Health ...

Do you know all the reasons to eat breakfast? I am sure you have heard before that breakfast is the most important meal of the day. Most people will agree with that, yet still many skip breakfast every day. Today I am going to share with you 7 reasons you must eat breakfast for your health. I hope that this list will encourage you to eat a proper breakfast each morning.

## 1. Less Fat

Many studies show that those who eat breakfast every morning are at a healthier weight than those who skip breakfast. Studies also show that those who skip breakfast have a higher craving for carbohydrates, which can also lead to weight and blood sugar problems. Do you notice a difference in your food cravings when you haven’t eaten breakfast?

## 2. Eat More

Those who don’t eat breakfast are said to consume more sweets, drink more soda and skimp out on fruits and veggies. As you can see, one reason to eat breakfast is to maintain a healthier diet throughout the day.

## 3. Belly Fat

Studies show that people who eat breakfast every morning are less likely to develop fat around the belly. So if you are looking to achieve a leaner stomach you need to eat breakfast. I would try eating a healthy breakfast every day. Is that a good reason to eat breakfast?

## 4. Thinking

Did you know eating breakfast affects how you think? It is true. Students who eat breakfast earn better grades. One study showed that students improved English and Math test scores just by eating breakfast. The next time you have a big exam coming up, remember that eating breakfast is just as important as studying.

## 5. Oatmeal

Of course, you can’t just eat anything for breakfast. It is better to eat something healthy instead of a bowl of sugary cereal or a doughnut. A breakfast of oatmeal will help improve concentration and memory. If you ask me, improved concentration and memory is great reason to eat breakfast.

## 6. Eggs

Eggs are another great item to have for breakfast. Just like oatmeal, eggs are said to improve memory and concentration. They are also a great source of protein and help reduce cravings. In studies, people who ate eggs for breakfast consumed less calories throughout the day.

## 7. Good Day

It seems all these reasons to eat breakfast will improve your overall day. It is definitely easier to enjoy the day when you aren’t being plagued with food cravings, are able to focus better, and know you are living healthy.

I hope you will consider all these reasons to eat breakfast. Do me (and your body) a favor and eat a healthy breakfast every day for one week. Come back and tell me if you noticed any of the benefits of eating breakfast during that week. It may take more than a week to see any huge changes, but you should notice a few differences after a couple of days. Do you have any other reasons to eat breakfast everyday?
