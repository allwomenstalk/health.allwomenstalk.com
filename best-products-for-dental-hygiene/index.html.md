# 10 Best Products for Dental Hygiene ...

Since a trip to the dentist costs a small fortune even without the dubious pleasures of cleanings, fillings, and root canals, it's important to invest in the best products for dental hygiene. Although regular check-ups are still necessary, brushing, flossing, and irrigating will help keep those visits as painless as possible. When you take good care of your teeth, you're far less likely to suffer from cavities and gum disease. Think about investing in the best products for dental hygiene; even though some of them are costly, they're still less expensive than a filling!

## 1. Waterpik Ultra Dental Water Flosser

![Waterpik Ultra Dental Water Flosser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/health/2013/03/1_waterpik-ultra-dental-water-flosser_450x450.jpg)

Flossing is a vital but often overlooked part of dental hygiene. If you don't floss regularly, you run the risk of excess plaque, gum disease, and bad breath. Plus, you'll end up with food stuck between your teeth at the most inconvenient moments. If you hate messing around with flossing string, then a flosser is definitely one of the best products for dental hygiene money can buy. Waterpik is a well-known, reputable brand with a flosser that helps prevent and reduce plaque build-up, gum disease, and even gingivitis. It will make your gums healthier, improving their circulation, and it lets you get a deep cleaning right in your home. You can use plain water or your favorite mouthwash, and since there are ten settings and six tips, you'll be able to find the perfect technique for you. Soap.com has it for $49.25 or you can get it straight from Waterpik for $99.00.

## 2. Cordless plus Water Flosser WD-450 Water Pik

![Cordless plus Water Flosser WD-450 Water Pik](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/health/2013/03/2_cordless-plus-water-flosser-wd-450-water-pik_151x550.jpg)

You'll find lots of Waterpik products on here, including this flosser! If you've got crowns, braces, periodontal pockets, bridges, or implants, it's ideal for your at-home use. Owing to strong water pressure and pulsing vibrations, your gums get an amazing, stimulating massage that keeps them healthy while getting rid of bacteria, plaque, and debris. It's less bulky than the aforementioned flosser, although it only has four attachments. Waterpik and Target both have it for $54.99, but check out Amazon as well.

## 3. Philips Sonicare AirFloss Rechargeable Electric Flosser

![Philips Sonicare AirFloss Rechargeable Electric Flosser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/health/2013/03/3_philips-sonicare-airfloss-rechargeable-electric-flosser_600x600.jpg)

Philips is another great brand for hygiene products, and the AirFloss Rechargeable Electric Flosser is a top choice. It makes flossing quick and easy – so quick you'll be done in just a minute. You can fill it up with water or mouthwash, depending on your preference. The microburst technology works with either one, and while it's gentle enough for sensitive teeth, it will get your teeth and gums sparkling clean. Find it at Drugstore.com, Target, or Walmart for under $80.00.

## 4. BrushPoint Sonic Power Oral Care System

![BrushPoint Sonic Power Oral Care System](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/health/2013/03/4_brushpoint-sonic-power-oral-care-system_410x410.jpg)

If you want something a little less high tech and a little more affordable, the BrushPoint Sonic Power Oral Care System is incredibly easy to use. You can either use the flossing inserts or the small picks, which help with hard to reach places. The flosser gently vibrates, creating a delightful gum massage, and although the machine itself is simple, it's excellent for removing plaque and promoting healthy gums. It's even got a toothbrush attachment, so it's an all-in-one oral care system! You can find it at Target for just $11.99.

## 5. Panasonic EW-DJ10-a Oral Irrigator

![Panasonic EW-DJ10-a Oral Irrigator](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/health/2013/03/5_panasonic-ew-dj10-a-oral-irrigator_300x300.jpg)

Irrigators are like flossers, although they rely solely on water or mouth rinse. The Panasonic Oral Irrigator gently but quickly shoots out 1600 pulses of water or mouthwash every minute, so it packs plenty of power to clean and irrigate the spaces between your teeth. You can customize it between two speed settings, so that it's comfortable and effective for your specific needs. Drugstore.com has it for $39.99, while Amazon frequently offers it for about $29.73.

## 6. H2Oral Irrigator

It gets rid of plaque and debris between teeth, improves the health of your gums, and successfully battles bad breath. You can find it on the H2Oral Irrigator site for $22.95.

It's an affordable option as well, at $32.99 from Conair.com.

## 8. Philips Sonicare HX6732/02

![Philips Sonicare HX6732/02](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/health/2013/03/8_philips-sonicare-hx6732-02_333x439.jpg)

This rechargeable Sonicare toothbrush is excellent and promises to whiten your teeth in addition to promoting healthy teeth and gums, thanks to its thorough brush head. It's a pretty pricey option, costing $119.95 at Amazon and Drugstore.com and $103.99 at Walmart, but it's still less expensive than getting fillings or a professional whitening treatment.
