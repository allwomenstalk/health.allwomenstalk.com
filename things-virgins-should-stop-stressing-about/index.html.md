# 9 Things All Virgins Need to Stop Stressing about and Chill ...

When you're a virgin, it's scary to think about what sex will be like. However, there's no point in stressing over certain aspects of it. Listen to _Gurl's_ advice and stop worrying about these things:

## 1. If He'll Know You're a Virgin

![If He'll Know You're a Virgin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xt/uf/xh3blph4560c19a80f70c466396170_300x399.jpg)

There's no reason to hide the fact that you've never slept with someone. If he finds out, he'll consider himself lucky to be your first!

## 2. If You'll Bleed

![If You'll Bleed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ur/in/nkx2h7d2560c19a916c7a559461689_300x391.jpg)

If your hymen tears, there could be a little blood, but it won't be like anything out of a horror movie.

## 3. If "It" Will Fit

![If "It" Will Fit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ex/ym/smxgfznt560c19a83c63d063536675_300x399.jpg)

It doesn't matter how big he is or how small you are, because it'll definitely fit.

## 4. If You're Doing It Right

![If You're Doing It Right](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/js/r8/rmc4n5rw560c19a85385f221255962_300x399.jpg)

You can't be a pro your first time. Practice makes perfect.

## 5. If He Likes What You Look like

![If He Likes What You Look like](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rw/v2/d9aynk9a560c19a8a7e8d665202314_300x399.jpg)

As long as you've showered, he shouldn't mind what you look like below the belt. He'll just be happy you're stripping for him.

## 6. If It's Going to Be Good

![If It's Going to Be Good](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l7/g1/u4ei2b2m560c19a8ef3bc111837032_300x360.jpg)

Everyone feels differently about sex, but you won't know how much you like it until you try it.

## 7. When It'll Actually Happen

![When It'll Actually Happen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ks/x9/kvmxiq5w560c19a8eabed294759566_300x399.jpg)

Don't push yourself to have sex before you're ready. It'll happen when it happens.

## 8. How Much It Will Hurt

![How Much It Will Hurt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yc/bw/ex890dqz560c19a935648337785209_300x399.jpg)

If your man gets you excited enough for sex and goes slow, then you shouldn't feel any pain. Sex is supposed to be enjoyable, after all.

## 9. What Others Will Think

![What Others Will Think](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ye/uh/s1wcaou2560c19a9d5876552563330_300x399.jpg)

Your body is none of their business. Do what makes you happy and stop worrying about your reputation.

If you're a virgin, don't stress yourself out too much by worrying about sex. Do you worry about any of these things?
