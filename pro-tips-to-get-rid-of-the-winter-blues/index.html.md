# Pro Tips to Get Rid of the Winter Blues ...

Are you tired of the dreary winter months? You are not the only one! Learn **how to get rid of the winter blues** with this video!

As-soon-as you wake up every morning, open up your window blinds!

Having a lot of [comfort foods](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/low-calorie-comfort-food-to-fill-you-up-but-not-out/)? Have more fruits and vegetables!

Lack of sun? Take a vitamin D supplement.

You need to get in at least 150 minutes of activity each week.

Do not think you are in this struggle by yourself. 1 in 15 people suffers from the winter blues.

These suggestions should do the trick! I hope you like the video and think spring!
