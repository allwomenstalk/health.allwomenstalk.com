# 17 Super Cool Ways to Make Drinking Water Much More Fun 🤗 ...

I hate drinking water. There. I said it. There are ways that you can make drinking water suck less though. So, if you are anything like me, give some of these things a try!

## 1. Invest in a Water Bottle You Actually like

![Invest in a Water Bottle You Actually like](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vu/dy/urbdc98956a4ddfd4cbb2972191133_600x600.jpg)

Seriously, it makes all the difference in the world!

## 2. Set Goals for Yourself

![Set Goals for Yourself](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p1/af/ghem19vf56a4ddfd2d3b4073375414_500x712.jpg)

I did this and everyday, it's like a challenge to finish up my water by the end of the day!

## 3. Infuse Your Water with Fruit

![Infuse Your Water with Fruit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h3/ay/muv2rhxj56a4ddfdd9e3f728705559_600x804.jpg)

You've probably seen all kinds of people cutting up fruit and infusing their water -- IT WORKS! It has made me drink so, so much more water.

## 4. ...or Herbs

![...or Herbs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jo/ne/kk1g7ukr56a4ddfdade9e246666187_500x700.jpg)

If you aren't a fruit fan, herbs are good too.

## 5. … or Veggies

![… or Veggies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n7/vj/o1kmwm0l56a4ddfe3af15098569983_600x600.jpg)

If herbs are too weird, try some veggies!

## 6. A Straw Can Help Make It Fun!

![A Straw Can Help Make It Fun!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bz/rp/kfifobyk56a4ddfdee3a9125192818_500x725.jpg)

Seriously, sometimes a straw can make everything better!

## 7. Compete with Your Co-workers

![Compete with Your Co-workers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iy/fd/zsbux4n756a4ddfe35032307689605_600x888.jpg)

So, you can actually challenge your co-workers to see who can finish up their water the fastest. This helps me so much!

## 8. Eat Your Water

![Eat Your Water](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gd/bd/krym3k7s56a4ddfe540ff981186412_500x625.jpg)

Watermelon, cucumbers, they are about 90% water -- why not eat your water instead of drinking it?

## 9. Cut Your Water with Juice

![Cut Your Water with Juice](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/th/i9/w49ie38i56a4de08d3e39152923402_500x750.jpg)

YES! Like you did when you were a kid -- it tastes awesome!

## 10. Tea Anyone?

![Tea Anyone?](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cm/pg/ycuogvp256a4ddff77c20713643052_600x900.jpg)

If you don't want just straight water, try coffee or try, no sugar!

## 11. Mark Your Water Intake via an App

![Mark Your Water Intake via an App](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ua/ee/se8xlyg456a4ddfeee6e6598536169_464x693.jpg)

This is another awesome idea, drink some water and mark it off so you can see how much you are actually consuming.

## 12. Reward Yourself after You Drink Water

![Reward Yourself after You Drink Water](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ke/px/fireznoe56a4ddff33205348148605_500x625.jpg)

If you happen to drink your entire bottle? Reward yourself with a small, small treat.

## 13. Prep Your Water the Night before

![Prep Your Water the Night before](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xi/bg/zrydxebw56a4de0170350540867795_600x516.jpg)

If your are constantly rushing around in the morning, prep your water the night before!

## 14. Popsicle Anyone?

![Popsicle Anyone?](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xj/su/delyshye56a4de01619ce201671742_465x698.jpg)

In the summer, this is ideal!

## 15. Keep Water by Your Nightstand

![Keep Water by Your Nightstand](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g9/fb/y72ah38356a4de020e8ac917670310_600x750.jpg)

Are you constantly reaching for a drink in the middle of the night? Keep water there!

## 16. Chug, Chug, Chug

![Chug, Chug, Chug](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fa/df/s2kwkr1r56a4de01c6649271606519_500x623.jpg)

I do this all the time, just chug and chug to get it over with!

## 17. Invest in a Great Water Filter

![Invest in a Great Water Filter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i3/lx/mglky0qm56a4de025ab74261860444_600x600.jpg)

You definitely need to make sure that you have a great filter so that you can stop buying bottled water.
