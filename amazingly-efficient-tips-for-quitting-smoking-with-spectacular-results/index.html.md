# 9 Amazingly Efficient Tips for Quitting Smoking with Spectacular Results ...

If you’re a smoker, no matter if you’re young or old, if you’ve been smoking for several years now or you’ve just started, if you are a daily smoker or you just do this occasionally, I’m sure you’d want to know what are the most simple tips for quitting smoking that you may use when you’ll decide to give up this filthy habit. Everybody knows that smoking represents a real danger to your health and despite all those warnings, people still smoke. A lot of the smokers really want to quit that nasty habit but they just think that it’s too hard and simply give up. In order for this thing not to happen, I will give you a few very simple and amazingly efficient tips for quitting smoking that offer spectacular results every single time.

## 1. Don’t Fight This Battle Alone

One of the most efficient tips for quitting smoking I could give you is helping you realize that you don’t have to fight this battle all alone, and that it will be much easier for you to give up smoking with the help of your friends and family. Don’t be stubborn or too afraid to ask for help! I’m sure that people will be more that glad to help you. You can even join support groups or you could go see a psychotherapist, whichever option you prefer and suits you best. Just, remember that you’re not alone!

## 2. Set Goals

A lot of the people who smoke, know that they’ll have to quit because smoking causes so much harm for their health only that they just keep postponing that moment and simply avoid this topic. If you are determined to finally give up this annoying habit, you should set very clear goals. Milton Erickson, one of the most famous psychotherapists said that «A goal without a date is just a dream.»; so maybe it’s time to make your dreams come true.

## 3. Clean Your House

I know this may sound silly, but if you decide to quit smoking, it’s very important to have a clean, fresh breathing air in your home or at work or wherever you may go. To not give into temptation you should definitely avoid the smell of tobacco. That’s why you should start cleaning right away! Wash all your clothes, your curtains, drapes or other objects that may smell like cigarettes. Use air fresheners, incense sticks or scented candles!

## 4. Exercise!

If you want to give up smoking, you must be aware of the fact that by exercising you can significantly decrease the intensity of the symptoms caused by the lack of nicotine in your body. That’s why, every time you might feel the need to light up a cigarette put on your sports gear and go! Even the most simple things, like walking your dog or gardening could help you forget about smoking.

## 5. Eat a Lot of Fruits and Vegetables

I don’t know if you already knew this, but I found it to be very interesting: the scientists from Duke University advise people who try to quit smoking to eat as many fruits and vegetables (or even fat-free dairy products) as possible, because, besides all the nutrients that they’re containing, they will also give you the impression that tobacco has a very very unpleasant taste and thus, you won’t feel the need to light up a smoke. Very tricky and clever, I might say, and especially, very easy to do. Just give it a try!

## 6. Drink Lots of Water

A lot of the counselors and psychotherapists that help people give up smoking; advise them to drink a glass of water every time they feel the need to light up a cigarette. Apparently, that glass of water can work wonders because it can immediately push back your urge to smoke. So, give it a try! You will also benefit from the other wonderful advantages of drinking a lot of water every day.

## 7. Brush Your Teeth More Often

This particular tip has, besides helping you quit this nasty habit, all sorts of amazing benefits towards obtaining a great oral hygiene. Try brushing your teeth every time you eat something, whether it’s a snack or a meal and that minty flavor of your toothpaste will not allow you to smoke your usual after-lunch cigarette. This works like a charm every single time!

## 8. Mint Candy and Chewing-Gum

Whenever you feel the need to smoke, you can try having a mint candy or a mint chewing gum and you will see how quickly will disappear that burning desire to smoke. You should be careful not to chew too much mint gum because it may cause you gastric disorders or it can have a laxative effect and you wouldn’t want that. So, use this tip wisely and don’t forget about moderation!

## 9. Keep Trying!

Every smoker has tried more than once to quit this filthy habit with no luck. If you are one of them, you shouldn’t despair! Some things need time and a lot of work, so don’t ever give up and keep trying if you haven’t succeeded giving up smoking from the first attempt. Try to figure out what went wrong, then try to fix it and be careful not to do the same mistake the next time you try to quit smoking and you’ll surely reach your goal. Just keep on trying and never give up! You can do it!

There are so many reasons to give up smoking! Once you’ll succeed quitting this nasty habit, you’ll see that all that hard work paid off and the results will definitely worth all that effort. So, did you use to smoke? What did you do to give that up? What other efficient tips for quitting smoking do you know? Please share your advice in the comments section!
