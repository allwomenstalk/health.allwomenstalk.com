# Smart Ways 🤓 to Improve Your Mental Health ...

Looking after your mental health is as important as having a healthy, balanced diet and exercising regularly. Actually, those three things have a symbiotic relationship – they all live together to make you a happy, healthy person – so it’s essential that you give focus to all of them. Your mental health is probably the one you give least attention to but you can change that with a few smart moves:

## 1. Make Your Bed

![Make Your Bed](https://img.allw.mn/content/od/xz/cw2hladd57210dcb9cc6d642644223_420x236.gif)

Make the effort to make your bed every day after you get up. It can help to de-clutter your mind and give you a fresh perspective at the end of every evening.

## 2. Prioritize Yourself

![Prioritize Yourself](https://img.allw.mn/content/ey/rq/thidao4r5721101fe86ec886659240_500x264.gif)

Stop putting the needs of others before your own and avoid getting in to situations where you are pleasing others to your own detriment.

## 3. New Hobby

![New Hobby](https://img.allw.mn/content/x8/kf/w0hw86ry572115be1de06079722660_500x256.gif)

Take up a new hobby whose only purpose is to give you a few hours of peace and enjoyment.

## 4. Treat Budget

![Treat Budget](https://img.allw.mn/content/td/ah/tw7dokue5721165ee7f96334358685_245x138.gif)

If you can, save a little money each month so that you can reward yourself with a few indulgent treats.

## 5. Less Negativity

![Less Negativity](https://img.allw.mn/content/bn/k0/smlnkqc4572116bae73c9048209492_376x203.gif)

Make a conscious effort to be more optimistic in your everyday life, and that includes cutting out those negative talks in the mirror every morning.

## 6. Memory Jar

![Memory Jar](https://img.allw.mn/content/ak/r2/yrlv98wg572113b7c92db725361706_500x273.gif)

Compile a memory jar by writing down fun things that have happened to you. It can make for a great mood boost to read through when you are feeling down.

## 7. End Bad Relationships

![End Bad Relationships](https://img.allw.mn/content/wj/cf/fhf07q9i572117b518e8e627587851_500x255.gif)

It’s time to cut ties with any friends, partners or colleagues that serve no other purpose than to make you feel bad about yourself.

## 8. Eliminate ‘Should’

![Eliminate ‘Should’](https://img.allw.mn/content/cl/wa/gygg38s9572118d963514639320441_624x230.gif)

Cut out the word ‘should’ from your vocabulary. It will make you more decisive and more open to trying new things instead of just wishing that you had.

## 9. Therapy

![Therapy](https://img.allw.mn/content/mg/dt/mnln0aq75721190c3c593677733894_460x258.gif)

There is absolutely no stigma in seeking out professional help if you want to address a deeper problem. That is what therapists are there for!

## 10. Say No

![Say No](https://img.allw.mn/content/lh/ad/a17n8279572119a24fab0156289884_500x225.gif)

Have the conviction and confidence to be able to say no to things you don’t want to do without having to come up with a lame excuse every time.

## 11. Less Complaining

![Less Complaining](https://img.allw.mn/content/n9/lp/awmbxog05721198fd18fe889147290_540x304.gif)

Try to eliminate a little bit of self-pity by making the effort not to complain about situations that are completely out of your control. It is a waste of energy.

## 12. Respect Your Pain

![Respect Your Pain](https://img.allw.mn/content/fw/n7/jxdp6n6u57211c2db0b9a956141630_400x208.gif)

Treat your emotional pain in just the same way you treat your physical pain. You wouldn’t ignore a broken arm, so why should you ignore a suffering mind?

## 13. Sleep

![Sleep](https://img.allw.mn/content/md/gm/qrp5fqob57211c7034e98353807554_350x197.gif)

Make sure you get in to a good, regular sleeping pattern. Getting the recommended seven to eight hours every night can do your mind the world of good.

## 14. Journal

Start a journal as a way to express your thought and feelings rather than bottling them up inside. You can also then read back to previous months to get some perspective if you need to.

## 15. Don’t Compare

![Don’t Compare](https://img.allw.mn/content/g5/lq/eq2ll5jh572111d299021249733073_500x224.gif)

We are all unique individuals; it does absolutely no good to be comparing your life to the lives of those around you.

## 16. Alone Time

![Alone Time](https://img.allw.mn/content/ej/tu/eqq1qfr7572111171b671754791005_500x221.gif)

Spend a bit more time focusing just on yourself; it can be draining to be constantly around people who require things of you.

## 17. Positivity

![Positivity](https://img.allw.mn/content/ji/xd/y33de8ea57211211b660f033495430_245x175.gif)

Start every single day by reminding yourself of something positive about your life, no matter how big or small it might be.

## 18. Less Social Media

![Less Social Media](https://img.allw.mn/content/i6/ld/semw57yc57211d1d1bb6b158347217_500x280.gif)

Try to spend less time on social media, as that is a prime environment for the temptation to compare your life to other people’s lives.

## 19. Share

![Share](https://img.allw.mn/content/b3/lr/u07qzxwi5721115797d2c587109449_500x272.gif)

Don’t keep your feeling locked up inside. Share your troubles with your friends and you will find that they will do anything to help you.
