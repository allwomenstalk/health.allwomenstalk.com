# 10 Best Essential Oils to Use ...

There's a lot of speculation about which companies have the best essential oils with the most benefits. To me, doTERRA an outstanding essential oil company! Their oils are certified pure therapeutic grade or CPTG, meaning that doTERRA protects the chemical compound of the plant just as it was produced from earth. No harsh chemicals, no substitutions, no fillers; they're simply pure, natural, safe and effective oils! I'm here to share with you my favorite 10 best essential oils by doTERRA!

## 1. Lavender

![Lavender](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yy/ro/pm1692fe_600x706.jpg)

Of course, first on my list of top 10 best essential oils has to be lavender! Lavender has many known benefits and it's my go-to for my children as well! This oil is widely used for its calming and relaxing qualities, soothing occasional skin irritations and easing muscle tension. I love to use lavender on my kids to help them sleep peacefully at night: a quick few rolls of lavender on the soles of their feet, and blissful, peaceful sweet slumber!

## 2. Peppermint

![Peppermint](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o8/ef/q1oaajhn_500x625.jpg)

Ah! Peppermint is one of my absolute favorite oils! It's a hot oil, so you have to be very careful you don’t get it in your eyes! Ouch! The reason peppermint is one of my favorites is for its properties in promoting healthy respiratory function and aiding in clearer breathing. I also love to use peppermint on my temples and the back of my neck to relieve irritating headaches! The cooling effect of peppermint, and the sweet, fresh aroma quickly knocks the headache out of you!

## 3. On-Guard

![On-Guard](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wj/h3/xusnvq46_600x901.jpg)

One of doTERRA’s most popular oil blends, known as “On-Guard” is also known as the ‘protective blend.’ This particular blend is a known for its unique, proprietary formula to support healthy immune system function. Its combination of wild orange, clove, cinnamon, eucalyptus and rosemary offer a great boost to the immune system and can also be used to disinfect counter tops or clear the atmosphere by diffusing! I have about 3-4 bottles of On-Guard at home and use this on my children daily!

## 4. Melaleuca

![Melaleuca](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w4/b9/f3g5zwwq_600x600.jpg)

Melaleuca, also known as the ‘tea tree oil,’ is known for its many beneficial properties in cleansing the skin. This oil has limitless therapeutic applications. I love to use melaleuca on my kids for minor cuts and scrapes. It's a perfect disinfectant and antibiotic as well!

## 5. Frankincense

![Frankincense](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ic/vt/ynqz092f_600x902.jpg)

If you’re ‘crunchy’ like I am, or even ‘semi-crunchy,’ you might hear the saying, “When in doubt, use frankincense!” That’s because this precious oil has been around for centuries, dating far back to the Egyptian era! Like melaleuca, Frankincense has a variety of applications from relaxation, boosting the immune system, disinfecting the home, and even mood enhancement.

## 6. Breathe

![Breathe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q5/yl/zw29n7qq_600x901.jpg)

Another popular oil blend by doTERRA that I am a huge fan of is Breathe. And, you guessed it: it's the respiratory blend. It consists of laurel leaf, peppermint, eucalyptus, melaleuca, lemon, and ravesara, which together cleanse and soothe the airways. This specific oil is my kids' favorite because it helps them with coughs and colds! I highly recommend this oil for those days of runny, stuffy, sneezy colds! We love Breathe in our household!

## 7. Terrashield

![Terrashield](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oh/ck/ceb3r7bu_586x745.jpg)

Terrashield, also known as the insect repellent blend, does exactly what the blend states: it repels insects. One application of this blend will protect you from biting insects for up to 6 hours! I carry around Terrashield in my diaper bag, especially when I know we'll be outside! Unlike regular bug spray, Terrashield won’t leave you smelling like chemicals, nor will it make you feel sticky or icky!

## 8. Deep Blue

![Deep Blue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j9/v5/sntuen4h_600x711.jpg)

Do you have sore, aching muscles or joints? Then Deep Blue is the oil for you! This blend is known to relieve discomfort due to achy joints and sore muscles. This specific oil blend contains wintergreen, camphor, peppermint, blue tansy, German chamomile, helichrysum, and osmanthus. Instantly you can feel the cooling sensation of the oils penetrating through the skin and easing muscle or joint pain. It's truly wonderful, and it's my husband’s favorite!

## 9. Grapefruit

![Grapefruit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ym/bz/fpus65e2_500x500.jpg)

Now, I think it's funny how much I love grapefruit essential oil, but I don’t like the fruit. I love grapefruit essential oil because of its fresh, crisp scent and of course its beneficial cleansing and invigorating properties. Grapefruit is known to lift your mood, help reduce mental and physical fatigue, cleanse and purify and of course aid for oily skin issues. Who would’ve thought grapefruit oil had so many benefits?

## 10. Citrus Bliss

![Citrus Bliss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x5/b2/gize41de_600x815.jpg)

Last and certainly not least, to complete my top 10 best essential oils list is Citrus Bliss! This powerful combination of wild orange, lemon, grapefruit, mandarin, bergamot, tangerine and clementine (with a hint of vanilla) creates a fresh, crisp aroma and helps reduce stress and anxiety and cleanses and purifies the air! This specific oil is also known as doTERRA’s invigorating blend, and to me it smells like orange creamscicle ... it definitely makes me smile!

There are many reputable essential oil brands out there, like Young Living, Ameo, and many more, though I've personally found what worked for my family is doTERRA.
