# 7 Essential Oils for Your First\-Aid Kit ...

Bandages and gauze are an important part of any first-aid kit, but in addition to these basics, there are also some wonderful essential oils for your first-aid kit. Many essential oils have healing properties and can be useful for cuts, scrapes, stomach aches, and muscle cramps. Some oils even have antibacterial properties, while others can stop bleeding. Because essential oils do have healing properties, there are a variety of essential oils for your first-aid kit that you can choose from,

## 1. Geranium Essential Oil

![Geranium Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bx/sj/xwvta2zg_600x766.jpg)

Although it has a sweet, floral scent, geranium essential oil actually has some very powerful healing properties, making it one of the best essential oils for your first-aid kit. Geranium essential has the ability to stop bleeding by helping blood coagulate and restricting blood vessels. It is also the perfect oil for scrapes and cuts, because it has antimicrobial properties. To use geranium essential oil for wounds, mix a few drops with some coconut oil and gently rub it on the wound.

## 2. Lavender Essential Oil

![Lavender Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vu/xe/j73xfmrf_600x600.jpg)

Lavender essential oil is loved by just about everyone. It is very relaxing and can help you fall asleep. Aside from lolling you to sleep, lavender can also be used for first-aid purposes. It has many uses for healing, such as relieving spasms and inflammation. It also acts as an antifungal, and can be used for skin infections. Personally, I always use it for back pain. I rub a little into my back ache for some relief from the occasional spasm.

## 3. Peppermint Essential Oil

![Peppermint Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y9/wy/baqyxv82_400x300.jpg)

Peppermint essential oil is a great oil to have on hand for digestive complaints. It can relieve stomach cramps, bloating, and even nausea. All you have to do is rub it onto your stomach for some relief. While peppermint essential oil is great at relieving digestive complaints, it is also wonderful for headaches. Rubbing it onto your temples can ease a headache.

## 4. Rose Essential Oil

![Rose Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rj/a9/xznbvf3u_600x598.jpg)

Rose essential oil has a lovely fragrance that will remind you of walking through a rose garden. As wonderful as this oil smells, it is also wonderful for a first-aid kit. Rose essential oil is a great oil to use for burns. Mixing a drop with a little coconut oil and rubbing it onto your burn will give you some relief, and it will also help the burn heal.

## 5. Roman Chamomile Essential Oil

![Roman Chamomile Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kb/hv/j4fpxkmt_600x566.jpg)

If you are looking for a natural way to relieve rashes from poison ivy and poison oak, Roman chamomile essential oil is just what you need. This oil is great at relieving skin that is itchy because of poison ivy, poison oak, and other causes as well. In fact, a trial found that Roman chamomile essential oil was more effective than hydrocortisone cream at relieving itchy skin.

## 6. Eucalyptus Essential Oil

![Eucalyptus Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hs/z6/ozt4bfpe_600x355.jpg)

Eucalyptus essential oil has many different uses. It can be used as a natural insect repellent, and will even help heal insect bites. Eucalyptus essential oil is also wonderful at providing relief when you have an upper respiratory infection. Breathing in the scent will open up your nasal passages almost instantly.

## 7. Wintergreen Essential Oil

![Wintergreen Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iy/d5/twzv3999_600x766.jpg)

Wintergreen essential oil contains 85 to 99 percent methyl salicylate, which is the same chemical compound found in aspirin. As a result, it is a great pain reliever, especially since it has been found to have a similar effect as cortisone. As you can imagine, wintergreen essential oil is one of the best essential oils you can use for inflammation, spasms, and achy joints.

Every house needs a first-kit, and while you do need the basics, adding these essential oils into your first-aid kit is a wonderful way to provide natural relief for your family. You just want to be sure that you buy therapeutic grade essential oils, as these have the most benefit. Do you ever use essential oils for health complaints?
