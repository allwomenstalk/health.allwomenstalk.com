# 8 Convincing Reasons to Eat Breakfast ...

If you’re guilty of skipping breakfast on the regular, I’ve got eight compelling reasons to eat breakfast and start your day off right! I know you’re busy in the morning and you’d probably rather sleep in or spend that extra time primping, right? I’ve found eight great reasons to eat breakfast regularly that just might change your mind!

## 1. Boost Your Energy

Giving your body a big boost of energy to start your day is one of the most convincing reasons to eat breakfast! It’s been said that eating breakfast is literally breaking the fast from sleeping for 7-8 hours, so eating a healthy meal will help boost your metabolism and raise your blood sugar to get you going for the long day ahead!

## 2. Lose Weight

It might not seem like there’s a direct link between eating breakfast and weight loss, but that morning meal really makes a big difference! People who don’t eat breakfast will typically be so famished by mid-morning that they’ll grab whatever high-calorie food to satisfy hunger. A study by the Journal of Epidemiology found that those who skip breakfast are 4.5 times more likely to be obese than those who regularly eat breakfast.

## 3. Be Healthier

Eating breakfast gives your body a boost of energy, so it would make sense that it helps you to be healthier overall! Traditional breakfast foods contain many of the vitamins, fiber and nutrients that our bodies need on a daily basis. So if you’re skipping this important meal, you might be depriving your body of essential nutrients that it needs to power through the day!

## 4. Improve Focus

Have you ever noticed that trying to concentrate on an empty stomach is difficult? It’s hard to focus on anything when your stomach is growling and you’re literally running on empty! A study done by Harvard University found that those who ate breakfast were significantly more attentive and other studies have found that eating breakfast improves mental performance and problem-solving ability.

## 5. Prevents Overeating

We all know that eating a healthy breakfast will keep us feeling satisfied and full, but it also prevents us from overeating later in the day. If your last meal was dinner the night before and you don’t feed your body until mid-morning or later, there’s a much higher likelihood that you’ll end up eating more food than you need later. The American Journal of Clinical Nutrition reports that regular breakfast eaters consume 100 less calories per day than those who don’t eat breakfast!

## 6. Helps Lower Cholesterol

Lowering cholesterol might not be at the top of your list of concerns right now but it’s definitely an awesome benefit to eating breakfast! Eating breakfast daily can help lower your “bad” LDL cholesterol. Having high level of LDL cholesterol can cause atherosclerosis, which can lead to heart disease or stroke. See, it really is the most important meal of the day!

## 7. Boost Immunity

These days we can rarely afford to take a sick day so I’m always looking for easy ways to boost my immune system, and eating breakfast can do just that! A study done in the UK found that skipping breakfast was associated with greater susceptibility to illnesses like the cold and flu. Make your breakfast even more healthful by including fruits with lots of vitamins that’ll help ward off bacteria and fight infections!

## 8. Improve Strength

Eating breakfast is also helpful in keeping you going throughout the day and strengthening your body. A study published by The American Journal of Clinical Nutrition found that eating breakfast was associated with a better attitude towards work or school, as well as improved strength and endurance throughout the day!

I hope these many health benefits might have changed your mind about skipping breakfast. Eating breakfast doesn’t have to be a huge ordeal; you can do something as simple as eating oatmeal or eating yogurt with some fruit. There are too many mind and body benefits to eating breakfast that are just too good to pass up! Do you eat breakfast on a regular basis?
